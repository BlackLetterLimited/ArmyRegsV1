. General a. This regulation, DA Pam 670–1, CTA 50–900, and special authorizations by HQDA specify the only items of insignia that personnel may wear on any of the U.S. Army uniforms. b. The insignia worn by military personnel designates grade, branch, organization, duty assignments, skill qualifications, and prior Army service. c. When authorized by the commander, members of honor guards, color guards, and similar details will wear the prescribed uniform with authorized accoutrements and those accessories authorized in CTA 50–900 (see para 2–7c). d. Personnel will submit all requests for insignia designs to Director, The Institute of Heraldry, Department of the Army, 9325 Gunston Road, Room S–113, Fort Belvoir VA 22060–5579.
