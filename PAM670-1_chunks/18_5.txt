Insignia, accouterments, decorations, badges, unit awards, and appurtenances The following insignia, accouterments, decorations, badges, unit awards, and appurtenances are authorized for wear on the male white mess and evening mess uniforms: a. Aiguillette, dress (officers only) (see para 21–27). b. Branch insignia (see para 21–10). c. Decorations and service medals, miniature (see para 22–8). d. Grade insignia (see paras 21–5 through 21–7). e. Insignia representing regimental affiliation (see para 21–24). f. Service stripes (enlisted personnel only) (see para 21–28). g. U.S. badges (combat and special skill, dress miniature, and ID) (see paras 22–16 and 22–17).
