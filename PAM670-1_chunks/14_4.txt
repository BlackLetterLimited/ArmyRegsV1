. Materials The AGSU will be made of the following materials and shades: a. Coat. The coat is 55-percent polyester and 45-percent wool mock elastique, 8.7 ounce in heritage green 564. b. Trousers, slacks, and skirt. The trousers, slacks, and skirt are 55-percent polyester and 45-percent wool gabardine, 6.8 ounce in heritage taupe 565. c. Shirts, short- and long-sleeved. The short- and long-sleeved shirts are 75-percent polyester and 25-percent wool, plain weave in heritage tan 565. d. Headgear. (1) Beret (see para 20–4). (2) Garrison cap, Army green (see para 20–14). (3) Service cap, heritage green (optional). The service cap will be made of the same material and shade as the coat (see para 14–12).
