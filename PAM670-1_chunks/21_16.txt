. Insignia for warrant officer candidates a. Description. The nonsubdued WOC insignia consists of the block letters “W.O.C.” in gold-colored metal, 7/16 inch in height, with each letter followed by a period. The subdued insignia is the same design as nonsubdued WOC insignia, except it is black (see fig 21–134). DA PAM 670–1 • 26 January 2021 231 Figure 21–134. Warrant officer candidate insignia b. How worn. Warrant officer candidates wear their insignia as follows: (1) On the ASU and AGSU coats, candidates wear the insignia on both collars in the same manner as described in paragraph 21–15b.
