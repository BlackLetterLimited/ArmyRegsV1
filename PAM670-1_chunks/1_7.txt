. Classification of service and combat, utility, and field uniforms a. The male service uniform (Class A version) consists of coat, trousers, a short- or long-sleeved shirt with tie, and other authorized accessories. The Class B versions include variations without the coat. b. The female service uniform (Class A version) consists of coat and slacks or skirt (optional), a short- or longsleeved shirt with necktie (Army green service uniform (AGSU) variations only) or neck tab (Army service uniform (ASU) variations and mess uniforms only), and other authorized accessories. The Class B versions include variations without the coat. c. Class C uniforms are the combat, utility, field, garrison culinary, physical fitness, and other organizational uniforms.
