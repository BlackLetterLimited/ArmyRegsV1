. Shirt, short- and long-sleeved, white, maternity a. Design. The white maternity shirt will be made from an approved specification or pattern. b. General description. The short- and long-sleeved maternity shirts are a 65/35-percent polyester and cotton blend of stretch herringbone cloth with a durable press and soil release finish in shade Army white 521.
