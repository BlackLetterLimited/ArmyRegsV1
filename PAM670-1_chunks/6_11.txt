. Accessories The following accessories are normally worn with the FREE: a. A2CU or improved CVC coveralls. b. Belt, rigger, tan 499 (must meet flash and flame criteria for aircrew members performing aircrew duties) (see para 20–3a). c. Boots. (1) Hot weather flyers (coyote). (2) Temperate weather (coyote). d. Chaplain’s apparel (chaplains only) (see para 20–8). e. Gloves. (1) Nomex® gloves, or summer weight flight gloves (green/black). (2) Maximum grip NT (Nomex®), friction fighter. (3) Other gloves may be worn when not performing crew duties (see para 20–15a). f. Headgear. g. OCIE, as determined by the commander in accordance with CTA 50–900 or CTA 8–100 (medical personnel). h. Socks, fire resistant, foliage green.
