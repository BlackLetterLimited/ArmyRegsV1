. Insignia for U.S. Military Academy staff The U.S. Military Academy (USMA) nonsubdued insignia is the USMA coat of arms, 1 inch in height. The coat of arms consists of the shield of the United States bearing a Greek sword surmounted by the helmet of Pallas. The shield supports an eagle displayed with scroll and USMA motto in gold-colored metal. Permanent professors, registrars, and civilian instructors of the USMA wear this insignia in the same manner as described in paragraphs 21–12 and 21–13 for all other branch insignia (see fig 21–129).
