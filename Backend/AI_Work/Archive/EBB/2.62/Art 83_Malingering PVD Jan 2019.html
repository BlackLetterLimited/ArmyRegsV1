Article 83 – MALINGERING

Elements:

(1) That you had knowledge of (his) (her) (assignment to) (prospective assignment to) (availability for) the performance of (work) (duty) (service), that is: (state the type of work, duty, or service alleged);

(2) That (state the time and place alleged), you

(a) feigned (illness) (physical disablement) (mental lapse) (mental derangement), or

(b) intentionally inflicted injury upon (himself) (herself) by (state the manner alleged); (and)

(3) That you ’s purpose or intent in doing so was to avoid the (work) (duty) (service) alleged; [and]

NOTE 2: In time of war or hostile fire zone. If the offense was committed in time of war or in a hostile fire pay zone, add the following element:

[(4)] That the offense was committed in (time of war) (in a hostile fire pay zone).

Definitions:

The word "feign" means to misrepresent by a false appearance or statement, to pretend, to simulate, or to falsify.

"Inflict" means to cause, allow, or impose.

Infliction of injury does not have to be accomplished by violent means.

“Intentionally” means the act was done willfully or on purpose.

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1b: How did you know of your (assignment to) (prospective assignment to) (availability for) DUTY?

1c: Did you understand what you were expected to do?

2a: On DATE at PLACE, did you feign (illness) (physical disablement) (mental lapse) (mental derangement), and/or did you intentionally inflict injury upon yourself by ACT?

2b: Describe how you did this.
