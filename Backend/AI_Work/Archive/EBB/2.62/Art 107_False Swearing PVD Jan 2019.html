Article 107 – FALSE SWEARING

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you ((took an oath) (made an affirmation)) ((to an affidavit) (in __________));

(2) That such (oath) (affirmation) was administered to you in a (matter) (__________) in which an (oath) (affirmation) was (required) (authorized) by law;

(3) That the (oath) (affirmation) was administered by a person having the authority to do so;

(4) That upon such (oath) (affirmation) you (made) (subscribed) a statement, to wit: (set forth the statement as alleged);

(5) That such statement was false; and

(6) That you did not then believe the statement to be true.

Definitions:

An “oath” is a formal pledge, coupled with an appeal to the Supreme Being, that the truth will be stated.

An “affirmation” is a solemn and formal pledge, binding upon one’s conscience, that the truth will be stated.

“Subscribe” means to write one’s name on a document for the purpose of adopting its words as one’s own expressions.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE, did you make/take an (oath) (affirmation)?

1b: Did you do so at PLACE? Where?

2a: What were the circumstances?

2b: Were you suspected or accused of committing a crime? If so, what was the offense?

2c: Were you advised of your rights (Art. 31 & Miranda)?

2d: At the time that you made/took the (oath) (affirmation) were you informed that it was a sworn statement?

2e: Who advised you?

2f: What were you told?

2g: Did you take the oath before or after the statement was made?

2h: What was the form/content of the (oath) (affirmation)?

2i: Why did you give the statement under (oath) (affirmation)? Were you required to do so under the law?

3a: Who gave you the oath?

3b: Do you believe he/she was authorized to administer oaths?

3c: Why?

4a: Prior to making/signing did you discuss the oath or statement with anybody (counsel inquiry)?

4b: In the statement, did you say: STATEMENT?

4c: Did you make the statement freely and voluntarily?

4d: Did anyone force or threaten you to make this statement?

5a: Was any part of the statement false?

5b: Which part was false?

5c: What was false about it?

6a: When you made or signed the statement did you know it was false?

6b: What did you intend to do or achieve by making a false statement (mislead, trick, avoid punishment)?

6c: Did you violate the oath you took?

6d: Could you have told the truth if you had wanted to?
