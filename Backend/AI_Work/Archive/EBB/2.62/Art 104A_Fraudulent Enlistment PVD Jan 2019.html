Article 104A – FRAUDULENT ENLISTMENT

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you were enlisted or appointed in the United States (Navy)(Marine Corps);

(2) That you knowingly misrepresented or deliberately concealed (a) certain material fact(s) about (his) (her) qualifications, that is, (state the facts allegedly concealed or misrepresented);

(3) That you ’s enlistment or appointment was obtained or procured by the knowingly false representation or deliberate concealment; and

(4) That under this enlistment or appointment you received (pay) (and) (allowances).

Definitions:

“Enlistment” means a voluntary entry or enrollment for a specific term of service in one of the Armed Forces by any person except a commissioned or warrant officer.

“Appointment” as used in the specification means any method by which a commissioned or warrant officer enters into the service of an Armed Force.

“Material” means important.

“Receipt of allowances” includes the acceptance of money, food, clothing, shelter, or transportation from the Government. Items furnished to an accused while in custody, confinement, arrest, or other restraint pending trial for fraudulent enlistment or appointment are not considered allowances.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1: On DATE at PLACE were you enlisted/appointed in the United States (Navy)(Marine Corps)?

2a: On DATE at PLACE did you meet with a recruiter/personnel for Navy/Marine Corps?

2b: Who was that person?

2c: Did you provide that person with information pertaining to your enlistment/appointment in the Navy/Marine Corps?

2d: Was he/she seeking information about your qualifications/eligibility for enlistment/appointment/separation?

2e: Do you believe that he/she had a duty/responsibility to elicit information from you about your qualifications/eligibility for enlistment?

2f: What type of information was he/she seeking to obtain?

2g: Was the information you provided true?

2h: Did you conceal truthful information or provide false information?

2i: How so?

2j: Did you know at the time that you were concealing truthful information/providing false information?

2k: How did you know?

2l: Did you tell the person that you were concealing information/providing false information?

2m: Did you lead the person to believe that the information you were providing was true and accurate in all respects?

3a: Was your enlistment or appointment obtained or procured as a result of you providing false information or concealing truthful information?

3b: How did the false information you provided or truthful information you concealed relate to your qualifications to enlist or be appointed in the Navy/Marine Corps?

3c: Was this a waivable/nonwaivable disqualification?

3d: How do you know?

3e: Did you know that at the time?

3f: Do you have reason to believe that the person relied upon this information in deciding to enlist/appoint you?

3g: Do you think this information normally would have been considered by the recruiting officer/Navy or Marine Corps personnel?

3h: What date did you enlist/were you appointed in the Navy/Marine Corps?

3i: What was the term of your enlistment?

3j: How was your enlistment/appointment obtained/procured by the knowingly false representation or concealment of information?

3k: If you had told the truth (revealed the concealed matter). would you have been enlisted/appointed in the Navy/Marine Corps?

3l: How do you know?

3m: Did you deliberately provide false information or conceal the truth?

3n: Was this a freely made decision on your part?

3o: Did anyone force or coerce you into this behavior?

3p: Could you have avoided doing this if you had wanted to?

4a: Under the terms of your enlistment/appointment, did you receive pay and allowances?

4b: How much pay? Was that consistent with your rate/rank?

4c: What allowances did you receive?

4d: When did you receive these pay and allowances? Was it after coming onto active duty?

4e: Where did you receive the pay and allowances?
