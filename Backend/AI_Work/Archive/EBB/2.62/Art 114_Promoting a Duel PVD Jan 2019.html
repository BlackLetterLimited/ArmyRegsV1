Article 114 – PROMOTING A DUEL

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you promoted a duel between (state the names of the alleged duelers); and

(2) That you did so by (state the manner alleged).

Definitions:

"Duel" means combat between two persons for private reasons fought with deadly weapons by prior agreement.

A "deadly weapon" is one which is "likely" to produce death or grievous bodily harm when the probable results of its use would be death or serious bodily injury (although this may not be the use to which the instrument is ordinarily put).

It is not necessary that death or serious bodily injury actually occur.

"Promote" means to further or actively contribute to the fighting of a duel.

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE did you promote a duel?

1d: Why were PARTIES dueling?

1g: Why do you believe that?

1h: Do you believe and admit that WEAPONS were deadly weapons as I’ve defined that term for you?

1i: Why?

2a: Did you promote this duel as I’ve defined that term for you?
2b: How did you promote the duel?

2d: Did anyone force or coerce you to do this?
