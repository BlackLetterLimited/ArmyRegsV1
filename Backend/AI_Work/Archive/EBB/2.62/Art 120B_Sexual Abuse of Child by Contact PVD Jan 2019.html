Article 120b – SEXUAL ABUSE OF A CHILD BY CONTACT (for offenses occurring on and after 1 January 2019)

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused committed a lewd act upon (state the name of the alleged victim), by (touching) (causing _______ to touch) the (genitalia) (anus) (groin) (breast) (inner thigh) (buttocks) (_______) of ________ with ___________;

(2) That at the time, (state the name of the alleged victim) had not attained the age of 16 years; (and)

NOTE 2: One of the following two elements must be added, depending on the body part touched.

NOTE 2.1: Touching of the Erogenous Zones. If the touching is of the genitalia, anus, groin, breast, inner thigh, or buttocks, add the following element.

[(3)] That the accused did so with an intent to abuse, humiliate, or degrade any person or to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person.

NOTE 2.2: Touching of Other Body Parts. If the touching is of body parts other than in NOTE 2.1, above, add the following element.

[(3)] That the accused did so with an intent to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person.

Definitions:

“Lewd act” means any sexual contact with a child.

“Sexual contact” has the meaning given the term in Article 120, that is: touching, or causing another person to touch, either directly or through the clothing, the vulva, penis, scrotum, anus, groin, breast, inner thigh, or buttocks of any person, with an intent to abuse, humiliate, harass, or degrade any person or to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person. Touching may be accomplished by any part of the body or an object.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: Sometime on or about DATE did you engage in a lewd act(s) with VICTIM?

1b: Where did this occur?

1c: Who is VICTIM?

1d: Were you married to VICTIM?

1e: Describe what the lewd act was.

1f: Did you touch (or cause ____ to touch) (vulva) (penis) (scrotum) (anus) (groin) (breast) (inner thigh) (buttocks) of VICTIM?

1g: Did you touch directly or through the clothing?

1h: What did you use to touch (cause ____ to touch)?

2a: Do you know how old VICTIM was at the time this lewd act took place?

2b: How did you know how old VICTIM was at the time?

2c: Do you agree that VICTIM, at the time of these acts, was a child who had not yet reached the age of 16?

3a: What was your intent when you participated in or committed this lewd act?

- to gratify the sexual desires of someone? Who?

- to abuse, humiliate, harass or degrade some person? Who?
