NOTE: Applicability of this instruction after 1 October 2007. Indecent Acts or Liberties with a Child was deleted by EO 13447, 72 Fed Reg 56179 (2007). Use this instruction only for offenses occurring prior to 1 October 2007. Consult prior MCMs for the instructions for this offense.
