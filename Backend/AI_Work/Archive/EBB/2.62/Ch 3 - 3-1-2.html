3-1-2. JOINT OFFENDERS (ARTICLE 77)

When an accused is charged as a joint offender, the judge should consult Instruction 7-1 (Vicarious Liability), 7-1-1 (Aiding and Abetting), 7-1-2 (Counseling, Commanding, Procuring), 7-1-3 (Causing an act to be done), and 7-1-4 (Co-Conspirators) for assistance in drafting appropriate instructions.
