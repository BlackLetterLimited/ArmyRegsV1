. Shirt, short- and long-sleeved, maternity a. The short- and long-sleeved maternity shirts are precured, durable press, made from 75-percent cotton and 25percent polyester broadcloth in heritage tan 566. The shirt has an eight-button front, a straight-cut bottom, and turndown-style collars with shoulder loops for officers and no shoulder loops for enlisted personnel. b. Personnel may wear the short-sleeved shirt with or without the heritage green four-in-hand necktie. Soldiers will wear the long-sleeved shirt with a heritage green four-in-hand necktie when it is worn with or without the tunic. Soldiers may not starch or bleach the heritage tan 566 shirts.
