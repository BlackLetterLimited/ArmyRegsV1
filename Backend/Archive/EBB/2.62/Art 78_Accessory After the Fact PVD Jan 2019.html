Article 78 – ACCESSORY AFTER THE FACT

Elements:

(1) That (state the alleged offense), an offense punishable by the Uniform Code of Military Justice, was committed by (state the name of the principal) at (state the time and place alleged);

(2) That you knew that (state the name of the principal) had committed such offense;

(3) That you thereafter (state the time and place alleged) [(received) (comforted) (assisted)] (state the name of the principal) by (state the manner alleged); and

(4) That you [(received) (comforted) (assisted)] (state the name of the principal) in order to [(hinder) (prevent)] (his) (her) [(apprehension) (trial) (punishment)].

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE did PRINCIPAL commit an offense punishable by the Uniform Code of Military Justice?

1b: What was the offense?

1c: How do you know PRINCIPAL committed OFFENSE?

2a: Did you know at the time that PRINCIPAL committed the offense of OFFENSE?

2b: What was your relationship with PRINCIPAL?

2c: How did you know at the time that he or she had committed the offense of OFFENSE?

3a: Did you, on TIME AND PLACE ALLEGED, receive, comfort, or assist PRINCIPAL?

3b: Which one or ones did you do?

3c: Describe for me how you did so?

3d: Did you do so by MANNER ALLEGED?

4a: Why did you (receive) (comfort) (assist) PRINCIPAL?

4b: Did you specifically intend to (hinder) (prevent) his or her (apprehension) (trial) (punishment)?

4c: DISCUSS.
