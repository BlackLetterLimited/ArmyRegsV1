Article 93A – PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES WITH RECRUIT OR TRAINEE BY PERSON IN POSITION OF SPECIAL TRUST – MILITARY RECRUITER

Elements:

(1) That you were a (commissioned) (warrant) (noncommissioned) (petty) officer;

(2) That you were performing duties as a military recruiter; and

(3) That you engaged in prohibited sexual activity with ________________, a person you knew or reasonably should have known was (an applicant for military service) (a specially protected junior member of the armed forces who is enlisted under a delayed entry program).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE, were you a (commissioned) (warrant) (noncommissioned) (petty) officer?

1b: Which one?

1c: When did you obtain that status?

2a: On DATE were you performing duties as a military recruiter?

2b: Had you received training on prohibited activities with recruits?

2c: Did you understand your responsibilities and obligations?

2d: Explain.

3a: On DATE did you engage in prohibited sexual activity with RECRUIT?

3b: What did you do?

3c: Did you know RECRUIT was (an applicant for military service) (a specially protected junior member of the armed forces who is enlisted under a delayed entry program)?

3d: If not, do you admit you reasonably should have known?

3e: Did anyone force or coerce you to commit engage in prohibited activities with RECRUIT?

3f: Could you have avoided committing this misconduct if you had wanted to?

3g: Was this a freely made decision on your part?

3h: Do you believe and admit that your conduct was wrongful?
