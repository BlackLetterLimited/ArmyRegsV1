Article 112A – DRUGS-INTRODUCTION [WITH THE INTENT TO DISTRIBUTE]

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused introduced _________ (grams) (ounces) (pounds) (__________), more or less, of (__________) (a Schedule ___ controlled substance) onto (an aircraft) (a vessel) (a vehicle) (an installation) (used by) (or) (under the control of) the armed forces, to wit: __________;

(2) That the accused actually knew (he) (she) introduced the substance;

(3) That the accused actually knew that the substance (he) (she) introduced was (__________) (or of a contraband nature); (and)

(4) That the introduction by the accused was wrongful; [and]

NOTE 2: Intent to distribute alleged. Give the 5th element below if intent to distribute was alleged:

[(5)] That the introduction was with the intent to distribute; [and]

NOTE 3: Aggravating circumstance alleged. If one of the aggravating factors in Article 112a is pled, the military judge must also instruct on that aggravating factor as an element.

[(5) or (6)] That at the time the accused introduced the substance as alleged, (it was a time of war) (the accused was (on duty as a sentinel or lookout) (on board a vessel or aircraft used by or under the control of the armed forces) (in or at a missile launch facility used by the armed forces or under the control of the armed forces) (receiving special pay under 37 U.S. Code section 310)).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: (On)(Between) DATE ALLEGED, did you introduce DRUG onto (an aircraft) (a vessel) (a vehicle) (an installation) (used by) (or) (under the control of) the armed forces?

[Divers occasions] How often did you introduce it during this time period?

1c. Do you agree that DRUG is a schedule (I) (II) (III) (IV) (V) controlled Substance under the Comprehensive Drug Abuse Prevention and Control Act of 1970 (21 U.S.C. sects. 801-971)?

1d. Have you discussed this act with your defense counsel?

1e. After that discussion, are you satisfied that DRUG appears in schedule (I) (II) (III) (IV) (V) of that act?

2a. Did you realize at the time that you were introducing this substance onto (an aircraft) (a vessel) (a vehicle) (an installation) (used by) (or) (under the control of) the armed forces?

2b. What (aircraft, vessel, vehicle or installation) did you introduce DRUG onto?

2c. Where was INSTALLATION located?

2d. Was INSTALLATION (an aircraft) (a vessel) (a vehicle) (an installation) (used by) (or) (under the control of) the armed forces? Which one?

2e. How did you bring the DRUG onto the INSTALLATION?

2f. How did you know you were entering/coming onboard the INSTALLATION?

3a. At the time did you realize that the substance you were introducing was DRUG?

3b. How did you know it was DRUG?

3c. What did it look like?

3d. How did you obtain this substance?

3g. Have you and your counsel seen the lab results concerning the test?

3h. Are you satisfied that based on those lab results that the substance was DRUG?

4a. Did you have any authority to introduce DRUG onto the INSTALLATION?

4b. Did anyone force you to introduce it?

4c. Could you have avoided introducing it if you wanted to?

4d. Do you believe and admit that your introduction of it was wrongful, that is, without any legal justification or excuse?

5b. Did you intend to distribute it to another person?

5c. Who did you intend to distribute it to?

5d. How were you planning to distribute it?

5e. How was DRUG packaged, meaning was it packaged in a manner to make it easy to distribute to others?
