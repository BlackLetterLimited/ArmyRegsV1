Article 105A – FALSE PASS – WRONGFUL SALE, GIFT OR LOAN

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you wrongfully (sold) (gave) (loaned) (disposed of) (__________) to (state the name of the person alleged) (a certain instrument purporting to be) (a) (an) (another’s) (military) (naval) (official) (pass) (permit) (discharge certificate) (identification card) (__________), to wit: (state the terms of the instrument alleged);

(2) That the (pass) (permit) (__________) was (false) (and) (unauthorized); and

(3) That you knew that the (pass) (permit) (__________) was (false) (and) (unauthorized).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, did you wrongfully (sell)(give)(loan)(dispose of) DOCUMENT to PERSON?

1f: Why did you do this?
