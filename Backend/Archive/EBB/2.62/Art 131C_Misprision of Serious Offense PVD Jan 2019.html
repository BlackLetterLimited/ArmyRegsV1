Article 131C – MISPRISION OF SERIOUS OFFENSE

Elements:

(1) That the serious offense of (the murder of __________) (_________) was committed by (state the name of the person who committed the offense) at (state the place alleged);

(2) That you knew that the said (state the name of the person who committed the offense) had committed this serious offense; and

(3) That, thereafter, (state the time and place alleged), you wrongfully concealed this serious offense and failed to make it known to the civil or military authorities at the earliest possible time.

Definitions:

This offense requires an actual act of concealment. "Concealment" is any statement or conduct which prevents another from acquiring knowledge of a fact. This offense is not committed by the mere failure or refusal to disclose the serious offense.

A serious offense is an offense of a civil or military nature punishable under the Code by death or confinement for a term exceeding one year.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: At the time and place alleged, did PERSON ALLEGED commit a serious offense?

1b: What was the offense?

1c: Was it a serious offense, as I’ve defined that term for you?

1d: Was it punishable by death or more than a year of confinement?

1e: How do you know that?

2a: How did you know that (he/she) had committed the offense?

2b: Who is PERSON ALLEGED?

2c: What is your relationship to PERSON ALLEGED?

3a: After you learned that PERSON ALLEGED committed the serious offense, did you conceal this serious offense?

3b: Where and when did you conceal the serious offense?

3c: How did you do this?

3d: Were you questioned by civilian or military authorities?

3e: Why did you conceal this serious offense?

3f: Did you fail to make it known to civil or military authorities at the earliest possible time?

3g: Did you know at the time you were concealing this serious crime that you were committing an offense?

3h: Would you agree that concealing this serious offense was wrongful and unlawful?

3i: Did anyone force or coerce you into remaining silent and not informing the authorities?

3j: Could you have informed them at the earliest possible time if you had wanted to?

3k: Was concealing this information a freely made decision on your part?

3l: Do you believe you had any authorization or justification for concealing the serious offense?
