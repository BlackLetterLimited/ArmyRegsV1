Article 104A – FRAUDULENT SEPARATION

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you were separated from the United States (Army) (__________);

(2) That you knowingly misrepresented or deliberately concealed (a) certain material fact(s) about (his) (her) eligibility for separation, that is, (state the facts allegedly concealed or misrepresented); and

(3) That you ’s separation was obtained or procured by the knowingly false representation or deliberate concealment.

Definitions:

“Separation” means any method by which a member of an Armed Force is released from the service.

“Release from the service” includes any severance or disconnection from an active or inactive duty status.

“Material” means important.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: At some point, were you separated from the Navy/Marine Corps?

1b: When was that?

2a: In order to obtain your separation from the Navy/Marine Corps, did you provide information pertaining to your separation to government officials?

2b: To whom did you provide such information?

2c: Was/were he/she/they seeking information about your qualifications/eligibility for separation?

2d: Do you believe that he/she/they had a duty/responsibility to elicit information from you about your qualifications/eligibility for separation?

2e: What type of information was/were he/she/they seeking to obtain?

2f: Was the information you provided true?

2g: Did you conceal true information or provide false information?

2h: How so?

2i: Did you know at the time that you were concealing true information /providing false information?

2j: How did you know?

2k: Did you tell the person that you were concealing true information/providing false information?

2l: Did you lead the person to believe that the information you were providing was true and accurate in all respects?

2m: How did this information relate to your separation from the Navy/Marine Corps?

3a: Do you have reason to believe that the person relied upon this information in deciding to separate you?

3b: Do you think this information normally would have been considered by Navy or Marine Corps personnel?

3c: What date were you separated from the Navy/Marine Corps?

3d: How was your separation obtained/procured by the knowingly false representation or concealment of information?

3e: If you had told the truth or revealed the concealed matter would you have been separated from the Navy/Marine Corps?

3f: How do you know?

3g: Did you deliberately provide false information or conceal the truth?

3h: Was this a freely made decision on your part?

3i: Did anyone force or coerce you into this behavior?

3j: Could you have avoided doing this if you had wanted to?
