Article 134 – PROSTITUTION-PATRONIZING

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you engaged in sexual act(s) with [((state the name of the person alleged), a person) (a person whose name is unknown)], not you ’s spouse;

(2) That you compelled, induced, enticed, or procured such person to engage in (a sexual act) (sexual acts) in exchange for money or other compensation, to wit: __________;

(3) That the sexual act(s) (was) (were) wrongful; and

(4) That, under the circumstances, the conduct of you were (to the prejudice of good order and discipline in the armed forces) (and) (of a nature to bring discredit upon the armed forces).

Definitions:

“Sexual act” means any penetration, however slight, of the vulva, anus, or mouth by the penis, another body part, or an object.

Conduct prejudicial to good order and discipline is conduct which causes a reasonably direct and obvious injury to good order and discipline.

Service discrediting conduct is conduct which tends to harm the reputation of the service or lower it in public esteem.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, did you engage in a sexual act with PERSON?

1b: What was the sexual act?

1c: Who was PERSON?

1d: What was your relationship with PERSON?

1e: Was PERSON your spouse?

2a: Did you compel, induce, entice, or procure PERSON to engage in a/the sexual act(s) in exchange for money or other compensation?

2b: What was the money or other compensation involved?

2c: Explain how this arrangement came to be.

2d: Was (s)he receptive to this arrangement?
