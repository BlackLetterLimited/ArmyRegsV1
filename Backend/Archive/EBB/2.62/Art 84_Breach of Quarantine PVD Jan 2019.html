Article 84 – BREACH OF MEDICAL QUARANTINE

Elements:

(1) That a certain person, namely: (state the name of the person ordering the accused into medical quarantine) ordered you into medical quarantine, namely: (state the place alleged);

(2) That the person was authorized to order the accused into medical quarantine;

(3) That you knew of (his) (her) medical quarantine and the limits thereof;

(4) That (state the time and place alleged), you went beyond the limits of (his) (her) medical quarantine without being released therefrom by proper authority; [and]

NOTE 2: If the offense involved violation of a medical quarantine imposed in response to emergence of a "quarantinable communicable disease" as defined in 42 CFR § 70.1, add the following element.

[(5)] That the medical quarantine was imposed in reference to a quarantinable communicable disease (to wit: _______) as defined in 42 CFR § 70.1..

Definitions:

“Duly placed in medical quarantine” means that you, for medical reasons, were ordered by a person with authority to remain within certain specified limits until released by proper authority.

“Broke” means to go beyond the limits of a medical quarantine while it is still in effect.
