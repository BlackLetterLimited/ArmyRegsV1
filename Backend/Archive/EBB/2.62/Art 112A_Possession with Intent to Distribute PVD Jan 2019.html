Article 112A – DRUGS-POSSESSION [WITH INTENT TO DISTRIBUTE]

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused possessed __________ (grams) (ounces) (pounds) (__________), more or less, of (__________) (a Schedule __ controlled substance);

(2) That the accused actually knew (he) (she) possessed the substance;

(3) That the accused actually knew that the substance (he) (she) possessed was (__________) (or of a contraband nature); (and)

(4) That the possession by the accused was wrongful; [and]

NOTE 1: Intent to distribute alleged. Give the 5th element below if intent to distribute was alleged:

[(5)] That the possession was with the intent to distribute [and]

NOTE 2: Aggravating circumstance alleged. If one of the aggravating factors in Article 112a is pled, the military judge must also instruct on that aggravating factor as an element:

[(5) or (6)] That at the time the accused possessed the substance as alleged, (it was a time of war) (the accused was (on duty as a sentinel or lookout) (on board a vessel or aircraft used by or under the control of the armed forces) (in or at a missile launch facility used by the armed forces or under the control of the armed forces) (receiving special pay under 37 U.S. Code section 310)).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: (On)(Between) DATE ALLEGED, did you possess DRUG?

[Divers occasions] How often did you possess DRUG during this time period?

1b. Where were you when you possessed this substance?

1c. How much did you possess? How do you know it was this amount?

1d. Do you agree that DRUG is a schedule (I) (II) (III) (IV) (V) controlled Substance under the Comprehensive Drug Abuse Prevention and Control Act of 1970 (21 U.S.C. sects. 801-971)?

1e. Have you discussed this act with your defense counsel?

1f. After that discussion, are you satisfied that DRUG appears in schedule (I) (II) (III) (IV) (V) of that act?

2a. Did you realize at the time that you possessed this substance?

2b. “Possession” means to exercise control of something. How did you exercise control of DRUG?

3a. At the time did you realize that the substance you possessed was DRUG?

3b. How did you know it was DRUG?

3c. What did it look like?

3d. How did you obtain this substance?

3e. What, if anything, did the person who gave it to you, say about this substance?

3f. Was a portion of the substance ever seized by authorities? If so, was it ever tested?

3g. Have you and your counsel seen the lab results concerning the test?

3h. Are you satisfied that based on those lab results that the substance was DRUG?

4a. Did you have any authority to possess DRUG?

4b. Did anyone force you to possess it?

4c. Could you have avoided possessing it if you wanted to?

4d. Do you believe and admit that your possession of it was wrongful, that is, without any legal justification or excuse?

5a. What did you intend to do with DRUG?

5b. Did you intend to distribute it to another person?

5c. Who did you intend to distribute it to?

5d. How were you planning to distribute it?

5e. Did you possess more than you could personally use?

5f. What was the market value of the amount of DRUG you possessed?

5g. How was DRUG packaged, meaning was it packaged in a manner to make it easy to distribute to others?

6a. At the time you possessed DRUG, were you [insert aggravating factor here]?
