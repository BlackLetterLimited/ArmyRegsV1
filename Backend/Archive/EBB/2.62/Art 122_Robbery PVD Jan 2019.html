Article 122 – ROBBERY

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you wrongfully took (state the property allegedly taken) (from the person) (from the possession and in the presence) of (state the name of the person allegedly robbed);

(2) That the taking was against the will of (state the name of the person allegedly robbed);

(3) That the taking was by means of (force) (violence) (force and violence) (and) (or) (putting him/her in fear of:

(a) (immediate) (future) injury to (his/her person) (the person of a relative) (the person of a member of his/her family) (the person of anyone in his/her company at the time of the alleged robbery) [and/or]

(b) (immediate) (future) injury to (his/her property) (the property of a relative) (the property of a member of his/her family) (the property of anyone in his/her company at the time of the alleged robbery);

(4) That the property belonged to (state the name of the person allegedly robbed);

(5) That the property was of a value of $__________ (or of some lesser value, in which case the finding should be in the lesser amount); [and]

NOTE 2: Use of dangerous weapon alleged. If the specification alleges that the robbery was committed with a dangerous weapon, add the seventh element below:

[(6)] That the means of force or violence or putting in fear was a dangerous weapon, to wit: (firearm) (knife) (_________).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On or about DATE, did you take PROPERTY from the possession of and in the presence of VICTIM?

1b: Where did you do this?

1c: How did you do this?

2a: Was the taking against the will of VICTIM?

2b: How did you take the property against the will of VICTIM?

2c: Did VICTIM do or say anything that indicated you were taking PROPERTY against his/her will?

2d: Did VICTIM in anyway consent to you taking PROPERTY?

2e: Did you think you had any legal authority or permission to take PROPERTY?

3a: Did you use force to cause VICTIM to surrender PROPERTY to you?

3b: Did you use violence to cause VICTIM to surrender PROPERTY to you?

3c: Did you put VICTIM in fear of immediate or future injury to: (HIS/HER PERSON); (THE PERSON OF A RELATIVE); (THE PERSON OF A MEMBER OF HIS/HER FAMILY); AND/OR (THE PERSON OF ANYONE IN HIS/HER COMPANY AT THE TIME OF THE ALLEGED ROBBERY)?

3d: Did you put VICTIM in fear of immediate or future injury to: (HIS/HER PROPERTY); (THE PROPERTY OF A RELATIVE); (THE PROPERTY OF A MEMBER OF HIS/HER FAMILY); (THE PROPERTY OF ANYONE IN HIS/HER COMPANY AT THE TIME OF THE ALLEGED ROBBERY)?

3e: How did you do this?

3f: Who was the person or what was the property the victim was afraid you would injure?

3g: How do you know VICTIM was in fear of immediate or future injury to his/her person or the person or property?

3h: Did VICTIM specifically do or say anything that indicated he/she was in fear of you?

4a: Did PROPERTY belong to VICTIM?

4b: How do you know this? Did you know this at the time you took PROPERTY?

4c: Did anyone or anything give you authority to take the property in the manner in which you took it?

4d: Did anyone or anything coerce you into taking the property in the manner in which you took it?

4e: Could you have avoided taking it if you had wanted to?

5a: Was PROPERTY of some value?

5b: How much value?

5c: How do you know the value? Did you know the value at the time you took PROPERTY?

[If alleged:

6a: Did you use a dangerous weapon to take the PROPERTY?

6b: What type of weapon did you use to take the PROPERTY?

6c: How did you use the weapon to put VICTIM in fear of immediate or future injury to his/her person or property or the person or property you previously described?
