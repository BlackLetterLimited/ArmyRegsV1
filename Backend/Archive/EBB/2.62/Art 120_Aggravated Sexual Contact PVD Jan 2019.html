Article 120 – AGGRAVATED SEXUAL CONTACT

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you [committed sexual contact upon] [caused a sexual contact to be committed upon] (state name of the alleged victim), by (touching) (caused ________ to touch), directly or through the clothing, the (vulva) (penis) (scrotum) (anus) (groin) (breast) (inner thigh) (buttocks) (____) of _______;

(2) That you did so by:

(a) using unlawful force against (state the name of the alleged victim) [, to wit: (if alleged in the specification, state the force used)];

(b) using force causing or likely to cause death or grievous bodily harm to (state the name of the person alleged) [, to wit: (if alleged in the specification, state the force used)];

(c) threatening or placing (state the name of the alleged victim) in fear that (state the name of the person alleged) would be subjected to death, grievous bodily harm, or kidnapping;

(d) first rendering (state the name of the alleged victim) unconscious;

(e) administering to (state the name of the alleged victim) a drug, intoxicant, or other similar substance by force or threat of force, or without the knowledge or consent of (state the name of the alleged victim), thereby substantially impairing the ability of (state the name of the alleged victim) to appraise or control his/her conduct; and

(3) That you did so with an intent to abuse, humiliate, harass or degrade any person, or to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On or about DATE, did you commit a sexual contact upon VICTIM?

1b: What specifically did you do?

1c: Did you touch or cause someone to touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, scrotum, anus, groin, breast, inner thigh, or buttocks?

1d: With what part of the body or with what object did you touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc.?

1e. Did you touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc. directly or through his/her clothing?

[Unlawful Force or Force likely to cause death or Grievous Bodily Harm]

2a. Did you use force to commit this sexual conduct?

2b. Describe the force you used to commit the sexual conduct. Were any weapons involved? Do you believe the force caused or was likely to cause death or grievous bodily harm? What sort of harm do you believe may have resulted?

2c. Was this force sufficient to either compel or overcome resistance to the sexual conduct by VICTIM?

2d. Who was this force applied to?

2e. Do you agree that this use of force made it possible for you to engage in sexual contact with VICTIM?

2e. Did you have any legal justification or excuse for using this force?

[Threatening or placing a person in fear]

2a. Did you threaten the victim in any way?

2b. How did you do this?

2c. What was your tone of voice?

2d. Did you specifically threaten to kill anyone, kidnap anyone, or commit grievous bodily harm to anyone?

2e. Which one?

2f. Did you intend the other person to interpret this as a threat?

2g. Why did you make this threat?

2h. Do you agree that this threat made it possible for you to engage in sexual contact with VICTIM?

[Rendering the victim unconscious]

2a. Was the victim conscious during the sexual conduct?

2b. How did he/she become unconscious?

2c. Were you the person that caused this unconsciousness?

2d. How did you cause VICTIM to become unconscious?

2e. Do you agree that rendering VICTIM unconscious made it possible for you to engage in sexual contact with VICTIM?

[Administering a Drug or Intoxicant]

2a. Did you administer a drug, intoxicant, or other similar substance to VICTIM?

2b. Which one?

2c. How did you administer the substance?

2d. Did you force VICTIM or threaten to force VICTIM to take the substance?

2e. Did VICTIM know that he/she was taking the substance?

2f. Did VICTIM consent to taking the substance?

2g. What sort of effects did the substance have on VICTIM?

2h. Do you believe that the substance made VICTIM unable to substantially appraise or control his/her conduct?

2i. Why do you believe this?

2j. Do you agree that administering this substance to VICTIM made it possible for you to engage in sexual contact with VICTIM?

3a: What was your intent in touching VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc.?

3b: Did you specifically intend to abuse, humiliate, or degrade any person or arouse or to gratify the sexual desire of any person?

3c: Which one?

4a: Did anyone or anything force or coerce you to commit this sexual conduct?

4b: Could you have avoided touching VICTIM if you had wanted to?

4c: Do you believe you have any legal justification or excuse for committing this conduct?
