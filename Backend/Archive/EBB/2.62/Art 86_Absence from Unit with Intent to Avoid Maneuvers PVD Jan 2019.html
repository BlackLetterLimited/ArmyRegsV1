Article 86 – ABSENCE FROM UNIT WITH INTENT TO AVOID MANEUVERS

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused went from or remained absent from (his) (her) (unit) (organization) (place of duty at which (he) (she) was required to be), that is, (state the name of unit, organization, or place of duty);

(2) That this absence was without proper authority from someone who could give the accused leave;

(3) That the accused remained absent until (state the date of alleged termination of absence);

(4) That the accused knew that the absence would occur during (a part of) a period of (maneuvers) (field exercises) in which (he) (she) was required to participate; and

(5) That the accused intended by (his) (her) absence to avoid all (or part) of the period of such (maneuvers) (field exercises).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a. What was your unit on DATE1?

1b. Where was it located?

1c. Did you have a duty to be with UNIT on that date? How did you know you had a duty to be with UNIT?

1d. Were you with your unit on DATE1?

1e. How did your absence from UNIT begin?

1f. Where were you?

1g. Why weren't you with your unit on DATE1?

1h. Was it a freely made decision on your part to leave or not return to your unit?

1i. Could you have been with UNIT on DATE1 if you had wanted to?

1j. Did you realize at the time that you were commencing a period of UA?

2a. Did you have permission from anyone in military authority to be absent from UNIT?

2b. Did you think you had permission from anyone to be absent?

3a. Did your absence end on DATE2? How?

3b. Did you have contact with anyone in military or civilian authority between DATE1 and DATE2? If so, explain who, when, how, and what was discussed.

3c. Did anything or anyone prevent you from returning to your unit before DATE2?

3d. Did you have any physical or mental infirmity that prevented you from returning to your unit?

3e. Could you have returned to your unit earlier than DATE2 if you had wanted to do so?

3f. Do you believe and admit that you were in an unauthorized status during the entire period from DATE1 to DATE2?

4a. Did you know that your absence would occur during a part of a period of maneuvers or field exercises? How did you know?

4b: Did you understand that you were required to participate in these maneuvers/exercises? How did you know that?

5a: Did you leave with the intent to avoid the maneuvers/exercises? Or did you formulate that intent at some point while you were away?

5b: Why did you want to avoid the maneuver/exercise?

5c: Do you believe your actions were wrongful?
