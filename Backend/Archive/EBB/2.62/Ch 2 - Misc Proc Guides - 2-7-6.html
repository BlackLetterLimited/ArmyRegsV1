2-7-6. PRETRIAL / PLEA AGREEMENT: OPERATION OF ARTICLE 58A ON A SUSPENDED SENTENCE

NOTE: In Army and Navy/USMC courts-martial, the appropriate instruction (from the two below) on automatic reduction in enlisted grade should be given. In Army courts-martial where all alleged offenses occurred on or after 1 January 2019, only provide the below instruction if at least one offense for which the accused is sentenced occurred on or after 28 July 2023. In Navy/USMC courts-martial only provide the below instruction if at least one offense for which the accused is sentenced occurred on or after 28 July 2023. Automatic reductions do not apply in the Air Force and Coast Guard.

(Army) MJ: Did you realize at the time you made the agreement, and do you understand now that, under the provisions of Article 58a, UCMJ, if (a dishonorable discharge) (a bad-conduct discharge) (confinement in excess of 6 months or 180 days) is adjudged, but suspended by the convening authority as provided in your agreement, you will automatically be reduced to the lowest enlisted pay grade, E-1?

ACC: (Responds.)

(Navy/USMC) MJ: Did you realize at the time you made the agreement, and do you understand now that, under the provisions of Article 58a, UCMJ, if (a dishonorable discharge) (a bad-conduct discharge) (confinement in excess of 3 months or 90 days) is adjudged, but suspended by the convening authority as provided in your agreement, you will automatically be reduced to the lowest enlisted pay grade, E-1?

ACC: (Responds.)
