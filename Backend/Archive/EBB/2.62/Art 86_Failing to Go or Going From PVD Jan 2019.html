Article 86 – FAILING TO GO TO OR GOING FROM PLACE OF DUTY

Elements:

(1) That (state the certain authority) appointed a certain time and place of duty for the accused, that is, (state the certain time and place of duty);

(2) That the accused knew that (he) (she) was required to be present at this appointed time and place of duty; and

(3) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused, without proper authority, (failed to go to the appointed place of duty at the time prescribed) (went from the appointed place of duty after having reported at such place).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a. At TIME, on DATE, where was your appointed place of duty?

1b. Who gave you the order/assignment to be there?

1c. How was this order/assignment passed to you?

1d. Did PERSON have the authority to give you this order/assignment? Why?

1e. What was PERSON’S rank and billet?

1f. Were you aware of PERSON’S status at the time that you received this order/assignment?

1g. Did this order relate to your military duties? How so?

2a. When did you receive this order to be at PLACE at TIME on DATE?

2b. Did you have enough notice to comply with this order/assignment?

2c. Did you understand this order/assignment?

3a. Did you go to PLACE at TIME on DATE? Why not?

3b. Did you leave PLACE after having reported? Did you have permission to leave?

3c. Were you late or did you not go at all? (How late?)

3d. Did you have permission or authority from any of your superiors not to be present at PLACE at TIME on DATE?

3e. Why did you fail to go to PLACE?

3f. Could you have gone if you had wanted to?

3g. Did anything or anyone prevent you from going to PLACE at the appointed time?

3h. Did you have any mental or physical infirmity that kept you from going to PLACE at the appointed time?

3i. Did you realize at the time that by not being at PLACE at the appointed time, you would be UA?

3j. Do you have any legal justification or excuse for this offense?

3k. Do you admit and believe that your conduct was wrongful?
