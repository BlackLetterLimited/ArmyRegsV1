Article 111 – FLEEING THE SCENE OF AN ACCIDENT-DRIVER OR PASSENGER CHARGED AS A PRINCIPAL

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you were the (driver) (passenger) of a vehicle which was involved in an accident;

(2) That you knew the vehicle had been involved in an accident;

(3) That you left the scene of the accident without (providing assistance to (state the name of the alleged victim), who had been struck (and injured) by the said vehicle), (or) (providing identification); and

(4) That you ’s leaving was wrongful.

“Accident” includes those situations where there is damage to property other than the driver’s vehicle or injury to someone other than the driver or a passenger in the accused’s vehicle. It also covers accidents caused by the accused, even if the accused’s vehicle does not contact other people, vehicles, or property.

“Wrongful” means without legal justification or excuse.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: Were you in an accident involving a vehicle?

1b: Were you the driver or passenger of this vehicle?

1b: Where did this accident occur?

1c: When did this accident occur?

1d: Who was involved in this accident?

1e: How did this accident occur?

1f: Were there any injuries involved in this accident? Who suffered them? What were they?

1g: Were there any damage involved in this accident? What were they?

2a: Did you know that you were involved in an accident?

3a: After the accident occurred did you remain at the scene of the accident or did you leave?

3b: How did you leave the scene of the accident?

3c: Why did you leave the scene of the accident?

3d: Prior to leaving the accident did you provide any assistance to (the alleged victim)?

3e: Prior to leaving the accident did you identify yourself to anyone?

4a: Was your leaving of the scene of the accident wrongful? Why?

4b: Did you have a legal excuse or justification for leaving the scene of the accident?

4c: Could you have remained at the scene of the accident if you had wanted to?

4d: Did anyone or anything force you to leave the scene of the accident without (providing assistance)(providing identification).
