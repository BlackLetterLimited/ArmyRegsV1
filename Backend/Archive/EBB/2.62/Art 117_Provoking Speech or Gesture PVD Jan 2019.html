Article 117 – PROVOKING SPEECHES OR GESTURES

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you wrongfully used certain (words) (and) (gestures) that is: (state the words or gestures allegedly used) toward (state the name of the person alleged);

(2) That the (words) (and) (gestures) used were provoking or reproachful; and

(3) That the person toward whom the (words) (and) (gestures) were used was a person subject to the Uniform Code of Military Justice.

Definitions:

“Wrongfully” means without legal justification or excuse.

“Provoking” and “reproachful” describe those (words) (and) (gestures) which are used in the presence of the person to whom they are directed and which a reasonable person would expect to induce a breach of the peace under the circumstances. Proof that a breach of the peace actually occurred is not required.

It is not necessary that the accused have knowledge that the person toward whom the (words) (and) (gestures) are directed is a person subject to the Uniform Code of Military Justice.

(Provoking and reproachful (words) (and) (gestures) do not include reprimands, censures, reproofs and the like which may properly be administered in the interests of training, efficiency, or discipline in the armed forces.)

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a. On DATE, did you use certain words and/or gestures that is: ACT?

1b. Did you have any legal justification or excuse for ACT?

2a. What was your purpose by ACT?

2b. Do you believe and admit that your words and gestures were provoking or reproachful, as I defined that term for you?

2c. Which One? Why?

2d. Do you believe that they would have the tendency to cause a reasonable person to respond with acts of turbulence or violence?

2e. Were these statements made in the presence of the person you directed the provoking/reproachful words toward?

3a. Did you know the identity of the individual to whom these words and gestures were directed? If so, how?

3b. Do you believe that that person was a member subject to the UCMJ? (how did you know)

3c. Did anyone force or coerce you into using the words/gestures?

3d. Could you have avoided using the words/gestures if you had wanted to? In other words, did you use them of your own free will?
