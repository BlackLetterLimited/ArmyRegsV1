7–8–3. CHARACTER FOR UNTRUTHFULNESS

NOTE: When a witness, including an accused who testifies, has been impeached by evidence of his or her bad character for truthfulness, an instruction substantially as follows may be given:

You have received evidence as to the (accused's) (__________) bad character for truthfulness.

(You have also received evidence of (his) (her) (their) good character for truthfulness.)

You may consider this evidence in determining (the accused's) (__________) believability.
