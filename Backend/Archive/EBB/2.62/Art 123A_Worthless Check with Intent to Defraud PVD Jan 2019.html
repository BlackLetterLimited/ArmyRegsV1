Article 123A – WORTHLESS CHECK WITH INTENT TO DEFRAUD

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you (made) (drew) (uttered) (delivered) to (state the name of the payee or other alleged victim) a certain (check) (draft) (money order) drawn upon the __________ (Bank) (__________), as alleged, to wit: (describe the check, draft, money order, or, if set forth in the specification, make reference to it);

(2) That the (making) (drawing) (uttering) (delivering) was for the procurement of any (article) (thing) of value;

(3) That the (making) (drawing) (uttering) (delivering) was done with intent to defraud; and

(4). That, at the time of the (making) (drawing) (uttering) (delivering), you knew that (he/she) (the (maker) (drawer) thereof), did not or would not have sufficient (funds in) (credit with) the (bank) (depository) for the payment of the (check) (draft) (money order) in full upon its presentment.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On date, at PLACE, did you, in fact, write and present to VICTIM, CHECK(S) as listed in the specification?

1b: Were these check(s)/draft(s) drawn upon your BANK checking account?

1c: Did you personally complete these check(s)/draft(s) on the dates alleged by writing in all of the information required as to the amount, date, payee and did you affix your signature?

1d: Please look at the copy(ies) of the returned check(s)/drafts, which are attached to your stipulation. Did you write these check(s)/draft(s)?

1f: How did you utter these check(s)/draft(s)?

2a: Were the check(s) made and uttered for procurement of something of value?

2b: Explain.

2c: What was the thing you were procuring?

2d: What was its value?

3a: When you made these checks, did you specifically intend to defraud someone?

3b: Whom?

3c: How did you intend to defraud them?

3d. Why did you intend to defraud them?

4a: Did you (place) (or) (maintain) sufficient (funds in) (or) (credit with) BANK for payment of the check in full upon its presentment for payment?

4b: Did you have your pay going into your checking account by Direct Deposit. Did you have Direct Deposit prior to DATE?

4c: Did you have any other deposits made to your checking account with BANK?

4d: Did you believe that these deposits would be enough to cover these particular check(s)/draft(s)?

4e: At the time you wrote these check(s)/draft(s) listed in this specification, did you know that you did not or would not have sufficient funds in your checking account?

4f: Did you have a line of credit on your checking account to provide overdraft protection for these check(s)/draft(s)?

4g: How did you know you wouldn't have enough money in your account to cover each draft when it was presented for payment?

4h: Did you believe that you had money in your BANK savings account which could be used to cover these check(s)/draft(s)?

4i: Were you maintaining a ledger for your BANK checking account during this period? If not, why?

4j: Did you anticipate receiving any additional sources of income other than your military pay during this period?

4k: Would you have had any way, based on what you knew or assumed then, of covering the check(s)/draft(s) written on your BANK checking account during this time frame?

4l: Do you believe and admit that you knew at the time that you would not have sufficient funds in the account to cover the amount when the check was presented for payment?

4m: Did anyone force or coerce you into committing these actions?

4n: Could you have avoided writing these checks if you had wanted to?

4o: Do you have any legal justification or excuse for writing these checks?
