Article 105A – FALSE PASS – MAKING, ALTERING, COUNTERFEITING, OR TAMPERING

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you wrongfully and falsely (made) (altered by __________) (counterfeited) (tampered with by __________) (__________) (a certain instrument purporting to be) (a) (an) (another’s) (military) (naval) (official) (permit) (pass) (discharge certificate) (identification card) (__________), to wit: (state the terms of the instrument as alleged); and

(2) That you knew that the (permit) (pass) (discharge certificate) (identification card) (__________) was false or unauthorized.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, did you (make) (alter) (counterfeit) (tampered with) a certain instrument purporting to be (a) (an) (another’s) (military) (naval) (official) (permit) (pass) (discharge certificate) (identification card)?

1b: Was the document falsely made or altered?

1c: How was this false document made or altered?

1d: Why was this false document made or altered?

1e: What was the false signature or writing?

1f: At the time you made or altered this document, did you have any legal justification or excuse for doing so?

1g: Could you have avoided making or altering this document if you wanted?

1h: Do you believe and admit that you conduct in making this false document was wrongful?

2a: At the time you made or altered the document, did you know that it was false?

2b: At the time you made or altered the document, did you know it was unauthorized.

2c: How did you know the document was false and/or unauthorized?
