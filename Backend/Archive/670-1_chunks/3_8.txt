. Distinctive uniforms and uniform items a. For purposes of applying the provisions of 10 USC 771, the following uniform items are distinctive and will not be sold to, or worn by, unauthorized personnel: (1) All Army headgear, when worn with insignia. (2) Badges and tabs (identification, marksmanship, combat, and special skill). (3) Uniform buttons (U.S. Army or Corps of Engineers). (4) Decorations, service medals, service and training ribbons, and other awards and their appurtenances. (5) Insignia of any design or color that the Army has adopted. b. Individuals will remove all distinctive items before disposing of unserviceable uniform items. c. Specific instructions regarding uniforms and uniform items are contained in DA Pam 670–1.
