. Aiguillette, dress a. Authorization. The dress aiguillette is worn only when personnel are performing duties as aides. Army attachés, assistant Army attachés, and aides wear the dress aiguillette with the Army service/dress uniforms, when prescribed. Personnel may wear the dress aiguillette with the mess or evening mess uniform only at formal occasions (when a bowtie is worn). b. How worn. See DA Pam 670–1.
