(1) The utility uniform subdued insignia is a black equilateral triangle, 1–1/4 inches long per side, with the letters “U.S.” embroidered, 1/4 inch wide and 1/2 inch high. The triangle is printed on a camouflage pattern colored cloth background, 3 inches long and 2–1/2 inches wide. If applicable, the insignia also indicates the designated assignment in black letters, 1/4 inch high.
