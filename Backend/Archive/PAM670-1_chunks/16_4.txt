. Materials The male blue mess uniform will be made of the following materials and shades: a. Male blue mess jacket. (1) Wool barathea, dark blue in Army shade 150, 14-ounce weight. (2) Wool gabardine, dark blue in Army shade 150, 11- or 14.5-ounce weight. (3) Wool elastique, dark blue in Army shade 150, 15-ounce weight. (4) Wool tropical, dark blue in Army shade 150, 9-ounce weight. (5) Polyester and wool blend gabardine, dark blue in Army shade 450, 9.5-ounce weight. (6) Polyester and wool blend in plain weave, dark blue in Army shade 450, 9.5-ounce tropical weight. (7) Polyester and wool elastique, dark blue in Army shade 450, 13.5-ounce weight. b. Trousers. (1) General officers. The trousers and jacket will be made in the same material and shade, dark blue in Army shade 150 or 450. (2) Other officers and enlisted personnel. The trousers will be made in the same material as the jacket, except the color will be light blue in Army shade 151 or 451. c. Braid. The gold braid on the trousers will be 100-percent polyester and produced by a certified braid supplier.
