Article 112A – DRUGS-WRONGFUL USE

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused used __________ (a Schedule ___ controlled substance);

(2) That the accused actually knew (he) (she) used the substance;

(3) That the accused actually knew that the substance (he) (she) used was (__________) (or of a contraband nature); (and)

(4) That the use by the accused was wrongful; [and]

NOTE 1: Aggravating circumstance alleged. If one of the aggravating factors in Article 112a is pled, the military judge must also instruct on that aggravating factor as an element:

[(5)] That at the time the accused used the substance as alleged, (it was a time of war) (the accused was (on duty as a sentinel or lookout) (on board a vessel or aircraft used by or under the control of the armed forces) (in or at a missile launch facility used by the armed forces or under the control of the armed forces) (receiving special pay under 37 U.S. Code section 310).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

[Divers occasions] How often did you use it during this time period?

1c. Do you agree that DRUG is a schedule (I) (II) (III) (IV) (V) controlled Substance under the Comprehensive Drug Abuse Prevention and Control Act of 1970 (21 U.S.C. sects. 801-971)?

1d. Have you discussed this act with your defense counsel?

1e. After that discussion, are you satisfied that DRUG appears in schedule (I) (II) (III) (IV) (V) of that act?

2a. Did you realize at the time that you were using this substance?

3i. Are you satisfied that based on those lab results that the substance was DRUG?

4b. Did anyone force you to use it?
