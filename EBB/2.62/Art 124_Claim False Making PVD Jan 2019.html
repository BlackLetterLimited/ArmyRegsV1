Article 124 – MAKING A FALSE CLAIM

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you made a certain claim against (the United States) (__________, an officer of the United States) for (state the nature and amount of the alleged claim);

(2) That the claim was (false) (fraudulent) (false and fraudulent) in the (state the particulars alleged); and

(3) That, at the time you made the claim, (he) (she) knew it was (false) (fraudulent) (false and fraudulent) in the (state the particulars alleged).

Definitions:

A "claim" is a demand for a transfer of ownership of money or property.

("False") ("Fraudulent") ("False and fraudulent") means intentionally deceitful. (It) (They) refer(s) to an untrue representation of a material fact, that is, an important fact, made with knowledge of its untruthfulness and with the intent to defraud another. The test of whether a fact is material is whether it was capable of influencing the approving authority to pay the claim.

"Intent to defraud" means an intent to obtain something of value through a misrepresentation and to apply it to one's own use and benefit or to the use and benefit of another, either temporarily or permanently.

"Making" a claim means the preparation of a claim and taking some action to get it started in official channels. It is an action by the accused which becomes a demand against the United States or one of its officers. "Making" a claim is ordinarily a separate act from presenting it. (A claim may be made in one place and presented in another.) (It is not necessary that the claim be approved or paid or that it be made by the person to be benefited by the allowance or payment.)

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: Did you make (each of) the claim(s) alleged in (each of) the specification(s)?

1b: Where and when did you make the(se) claim(s)?

1c: Were the claims made against the United States or an Officer of the United States? Against whom?

1d: For what, and for what amount, was (each of) the claim(s) made?

1e: How was (each of) the(se) claim(s) made?

1f: Did you do the ACT yourself (on each occasion)?

1g. [If answer to 1f is no, did you direct, or cause, the ACT to happen?

2a: Do you understand the definitions of the word(s) (false) (fraudulent) (false and fraudulent)?

2b: (Was) (Were) the claim(s) (false) (fraudulent) (false and fraudulent)?

2c: Why do you believe this?

2d: Why did you make the(se) false claim(s)?

3: Did you know on DATE that the claim(s) (was) (were) (false) (fraudulent) (false and fraudulent)?
