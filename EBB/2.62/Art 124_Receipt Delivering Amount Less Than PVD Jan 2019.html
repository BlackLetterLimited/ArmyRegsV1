Article 124 – DELIVERING AMOUNT LESS THAN CALLED FOR BY RECEIPT

Elements:

(1) That you had (charge) (possession) (custody) (control) of certain (money) (property) (__________) of the United States (furnished) (intended) (furnished and intended) for the armed forces;

(2) That you obtained a (receipt) (certificate) for a certain (amount) (quantity) of this (money) (property) (__________) from (state the name of the person alleged);

(3) That for the (receipt) (certificate), you (state the time and place alleged), knowingly delivered to (state the name of the person alleged) (an amount) (a quantity) of this (money) (property) which (he) (she) knew was less than the (amount) (quantity) specified in the (receipt) (certificate);

(4) That (state the name of the person who allegedly received the money or property) was a person who had authority to receive the (money) (property) (__________); and

(5) That the undelivered (money) (property) (__________) was of the value of (state the value alleged) (or of some lesser value, in which case the finding should be in the lesser amount).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, did you have (charge) (possession) (custody) (control) of certain (money) (property)?

1b: Describe the (money) (property) over which you had (charge) (possession) (custody) (control).

1c: Did the (money) (property) belong to the United States?

1d: Did you know the (money) (property) belonged to the United States? How?

1e: Was the (money) (property) (furnished) (intended) (furnished and intended) for the armed forces?

1f: How do you know that?

2a: On DATE at PLACE, did you obtain a (receipt) (certificate) for a certain (amount) (quantity) of this (money) (property)?

2b: From whom did you obtain the receipt?

2c: What was the (amount) (quantity) of (money) (property) on the (receipt) (certificate)?

3a: In exchange for the (receipt) (certificate), did you deliver to PERSON ALLEGED an (amount) (quantity) of the (money) (property)?

3b: What was the (amount) (quantity) of the (money) (property) you delivered?

3c: Was the (amount) (quantity) of the (money) (property) you delivered less than the (amount) (quantity) specified in the (receipt) (certificate)?

3d: Did you know that at the time?

3e: Did you knowingly deliver to PERSON ALLEGED (an amount) (a quantity) of this (money) (property) which you knew was less than the (amount) (quantity) specified in the (receipt) (certificate)?

4: Was PERSON ALLEGED authorized to receive the (money) (property) specified in the (receipt) (certificate)?

5a: Was the undelivered (money) (property) of some value?

5b: What was the value of the undelivered (money) (property)?
