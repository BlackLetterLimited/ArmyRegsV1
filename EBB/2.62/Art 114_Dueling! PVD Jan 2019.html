Article 114 – DUELING

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you fought (state the name of the person alleged) with deadly weapons, that is: (state the weapons alleged);

(2) That the combat was for private reasons; and

(3) That the combat was by prior agreement.

Definitions:

A "deadly weapon" is one which is used in a manner likely to produce death or grievous bodily harm.

A weapon is "likely" to produce death or grievous bodily harm when the probable results of its use would be death or serious bodily injury (although this may not be the use to which the instrument is ordinarily put).

It is not necessary that death or serious bodily harm actually occur.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, did you fight PERSON?

1b: Describe how you fought PERSON.

1c: Did you use weapons to fight PERSON?

1d: Describe the weapons you used to fight PERSON.

1e: Do you believe and admit that WEAPONS were deadly weapons as I’ve defined that term for you?

1f: Why do you believe that?

2a: Was this combat for private reasons?

2b: Explain for me why you believe that.

3a: Before engaging in this private combat, did you have an agreement with PERSON to do so?

3b: How did you and PERSON enter into this agreement?

3c: What was your agreement with PERSON?

3d: Why did you and PERSON agree to engage in this private combat?

3e: Did you have any legal justification or excuse for this conduct?

3f: Did anyone force or coerce you into doing this?

3g: Could you have avoided dueling if you had wanted to?
