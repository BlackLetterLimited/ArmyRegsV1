Article 103B – AIDING THE ENEMY

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you aided the enemy, namely: (state the name or description of the enemy who purportedly received the aid);

(2) That you did so with certain (arms) (ammunition) (supplies) (money) (__________) by (state the manner in which the aid was allegedly supplied).

Definitions:

To "aid the enemy" means to furnish the enemy with (arms) (ammunition) (supplies) (money) (__________), (whether or not the articles furnished were needed by the enemy) (and) (whether or not the transaction was a sale or a donation).

"Enemy" includes (not only) organized opposing forces in time of war, (but also any other hostile body that our forces may be opposing) (such as a rebellious mob or a band of renegades) (and includes civilians as well as members of military organizations).

"Enemy" is not restricted to the enemy government or its armed forces. All the citizens of one belligerent are enemies of the government and all the citizens of the other.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE did you aid the enemy?

1b: Who was the enemy you aided?

1c: What made he/she/them the enemy?

1d: When you did this, did you know he/she/they were the enemy?

1e: How did you know that?

2a: On DATE at PLACE, what did you do to aid the enemy?

2b: Describe the (arms) (ammunition) (supplies) (money) (__________) you supplied to the enemy.

2c: How did you obtain the (arms) (ammunition) (supplies) (money) (__________) you supplied to the enemy?

2d: How did you supply the (arms) (ammunition) (supplies) (money) (__________) to the enemy?

2e: Why did you supply the (arms) (ammunition) (supplies) (money) (__________) to the enemy?

2f: Did anyone give you the authority to do so?

2g: Did you have any legal justification or excuse for doing so?

2h: Was it a freely made decision on your part to aid the enemy in this manner?

2i: Did anyone force or coerce you to do this?

2j: Could you have avoided aiding the enemy if you had wanted?
