Article 124B – GRAFT-ASKING, ACCEPTING, OR RECEIVING

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you wrongfully and unlawfully (asked for) (accepted) (received) (the sum of__________ dollars) (__________, of a value of (about) __________ dollars) (__________) from (state the name of the person or organization alleged);

(2) That, at that time, you (occupied an official position) (had official duties), namely, (state the official position or official duties, as alleged);

(3) That you (asked for) (accepted) (received) this (sum) (__________) (as compensation for) (in recognition of) services (rendered) (to be rendered) (rendered and to be rendered) by (him) (her) in relation to (state the matter alleged); and

(4) That (state the matter alleged) was an official matter in which the United States was interested.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE did you engage in the offense of graft?

1b: Did you ask for, accept, or receive money or something of value?

1c: What did you ask for, accept, or receive?

1d: What was its value?

1e: Who was the person from whom you asked for, accepted or received money or something of value?

1f: What was your relationship to that other person?

1g: Do you believe and admit that you conduct, in asking for, accepting, or receiving the money or item of value was unlawful and wrongful?

2a: What was your title/official position/duties at that time?

2b: Where/how did this talk/meeting take place?

2c: What transpired?

2d: When you did this, were you occupying a position or performing duties?

2e: Explain.

3a: Why did you ask for, accept, or receive the money or article of value?

3b: Was it (as compensation for) (in recognition of) services (rendered) (to be rendered) (rendered and to be rendered)?

3c: Explain.

3d: Had you taken some action, or were you expected to take some action in regard to ALLEGED MATTER?

3e: Explain your official duties in regard to ALLEGED MATTER.

3f: And, how did the money or thing of value relate to your duties in regard to ALLEGED MATTER?

4a: Do you believe and admit that ALLEGED MATTER was an official matter in which the United States was and is interested?

4b: Why do you believe that?

4c: What, if any, authorization or legal justification did you have for this conduct?

4d: Do you believe and admit that your conduct was wrongful?

4e: Did anyone or anything give you permission or authority to do this?

4f: Did anyone or anything force you to do this?

4g: Could you have avoided doing this if you had wanted to?
