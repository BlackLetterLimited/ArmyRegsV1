Article 131F – FAILING TO ENFORCE OR COMPLY WITH CODE

Elements:

(1) That, at (state the time and place alleged), you failed to (enforce) (comply with) Article (___) of the Uniform Code of Military Justice regulating a proceeding (before) (during) (after) trial of ##an accused## by (state the manner alleged);

(2) That you had the duty of (enforcing) (complying with) that provision of the Code;

(3) That you knew that (he) (she) was charged with this duty;
and

(4) That you ’s failure to (enforce) (comply with) that provision was intentional.

Definitions:

Errors made in good faith before, during, or after trial are not punishable under this section.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE, at PLACE, did you have certain duties with respect to enforcing or complying with certain provisions of the code regulating a certain proceeding before, during or after a trial?

1b: Describe what those duties were?

1c: Who charged you with this duty in relation to the case against PERSON?

2a: Did you know you had these particular duties?

2b: How did you learn of your duties in relation to this case?

2c: Were you told or advised that you should proceed in a specific manner with respect to these duties?

2d: What was your understanding of the manner in which you were required to proceed?

3a: As part of your duties, were you required to be familiar with applicable provisions of the UCMJ? Were you familiar with those provisions?

3b: Was the manner in which you proceeded in this case in compliance with the applicable provisions of the UCMJ?

3c: Why or why not?

3d: Was your failure to follow the applicable provision of the code with respect to PERSON’s case intentional?

3e: Describe why or why not?

3f: What provision of the code did you fail to comply with or enforce?

3g: Was your failure to follow this provision(s) intentional?

4a: Did you understand at the time you were doing it that your conduct was wrongful?

4b: Why do you believe your conduct was wrongful?

4c: Did anyone force or coerce you into this conduct?

4d: Could you have avoided this conduct if you had wanted to?

4e: Was it a freely made decision on your part to engage in this conduct?

4f: Do you believe you had any legal justification or authorization?
