ELEMENTS

In specification __ of Charge __, you are charged with the offense of distribution of a controlled substance, in violation of Article 112a, UCMJ. By pleading guilty to this offense, you are admitting that the following elements are true and accurately describe what you did:

That at or near _____________, on divers occasions between o/a DATE and o/a DATE, you distributed DRUG;

That you actually knew you distributed the substance;

That you actually knew that the substance you distributed was DRUG or of a contraband nature; and

That the distribution by you was wrongful.

Definitions

In considering those elements, you should use and understand the following definitions:

“Divers occasions” means two or more occasions.

"Distribute" means to deliver to the possession of another. "Deliver" means the actual, constructive, or attempted transfer of an item. While a transfer of Drug may have been made or attempted in exchange for money or other property or a promise of payment, proof of a commercial transaction is not required.

To be punishable under Article 112a, distribution of a controlled substance must be wrongful. Distribution of a controlled substance is wrongful if it is without legal justification or authorization.

Distribution of a controlled substance is not wrongful if such act or acts are: (a) done pursuant to legitimate law enforcement activities, for example, an informant who delivers drugs as part of an undercover operation is not guilty of wrongful distribution; or (b) done by authorized personnel in the performance of medical duties.

You must be aware of the presence of the substance at the time of the distribution. A person who delivers a package without knowing that it actually contains DRUG is not guilty of wrongful distribution of DRUG.

It is not necessary that you were aware of the exact identity of the contraband substance. The knowledge requirement is satisfied if you knew the substance was prohibited. Similarly, if you believed the substance to be a contraband substance, such as cocaine, when in fact it is heroin, you had sufficient knowledge to satisfy that element of the offense.

A contraband substance is one that is illegal to distribute.

However, a person who distributes cocaine, but actually believes it to be sugar, is not guilty of the wrongful distribution of cocaine.
