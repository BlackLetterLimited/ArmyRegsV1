Article 114 – FAILURE TO REPORT A DUEL

Elements:

(1) That a challenge to fight a duel (had been sent) (was about to be sent) by __________ to __________;

(2) That you had knowledge of this challenge; and

(3) That (state the time and place alleged), you failed to report this fact promptly to the proper authority.

Definitions:

"Challenge," as used in this specification, means an invitation, summons, or request to fight a duel.

"Duel" means combat between two persons for private reasons with deadly weapons by prior agreement.

A "deadly weapon" is one which is used in a manner likely to produce death or grievous bodily harm.

A weapon is "likely" to produce death or grievous bodily harm when the probable results of its use would be death or serious bodily injury (although this may not be the use to which the instrument is ordinarily put).

It is not necessary that death or serious bodily injury actually occur.
