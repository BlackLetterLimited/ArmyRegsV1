Article 120 – ABUSIVE SEXUAL CONTACT BY THREAT, FRAUDULENT REPRESENTATION, OR ARTIFICE

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused [committed sexual contact upon] [caused ________ to commit sexual contact upon] (state the name of the alleged victim), by (touching) (causing _________ to touch), the (vulva) (penis) (scrotum) (anus) (groin) (breast) (inner thigh) (buttocks) (________) of _______ directly or through the clothing with (______'s (state body part) ((a(state object));

(2) That the accused did so by

(a) threatening or placing (state the name of the alleged victim) in fear;

(b) making a fraudulent representation that the sexual contact served a professional purpose;

(c) inducing a belief by artifice, pretense, or concealment that the accused was another person; and

(3) That the accused did so with an intent to abuse, humiliate, or degrade any person, or to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On or about DATE, did you commit a sexual contact upon VICTIM?

1b: What specifically did you do?

1c: Did you touch or cause someone to touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, scrotum, anus, groin, breast, inner thigh, or buttocks?

1d: With what part of the body or with what object did you touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc.?

1e. Did you touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc. directly or through his/her clothing?

[Threat or Placing in Fear]

2a: Did you make VICTIM believe that if he/she did not comply with your requested sexual conduct that someone would be subject to a wrongful action?

2b: What specifically did you threaten to do?

2c: How did you communicate this threat?

2d: Did you intend for VICTIM to believe that you would carry out this wrongful action?

2e: Who was the subject of this wrongful action?

2f: Did you specifically threaten to initiate an adverse personnel action unless the alleged victim submits to your requested sexual conduct; or threaten to withhold favorable personnel action unless the alleged victim submits to your requested sexual conduct.

2g: Which one?

2h: Do you believe a reasonable person in the VICTIM’s position would believe that you would carry out these actions if VICTIM did not comply with your requests for sexual conduct?

2i: Why is that?

2j: How do you know that VICTIM did not agree?

2k: Did you know at the time of the sexual conduct that VICTIM was only agreeing to the sexual conduct due to the threatened wrongful actions?

[Fraudulent Representation]

2a: Did you or someone else represent to VICTIM that the sexual conduct would serve some professional purpose?

2b: Please explain what the professional purpose was?

2c: Did you know that this representation was untrue at the time?

2d: Did you intend to deceive victim by telling him/her that the sexual conduct served a professional purpose?

2e: Do you believe that VICTIM was in fact deceived?

2f: Do you agree that VICTIM would not have allowed you to engage in the sexual conduct had he/she known the truth?

[Artifice -- Another Person]

2a: Did you engage in the sexual conduct by inducing in VICTIM a belief by artifice, pretense, or concealment that you were another person?

2b: How did you induce this belief?

2c: Did VICTIM in fact believe that you were another person?

2d: Who did VICTIM believe you were?

2e: How do you know that VICTIM believed you were another person?

2f: Did you know at the time that VICTIM believed you were someone else?

3a: What was your intent in touching VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc.?

3b: Did you specifically intend to abuse, humiliate, or degrade any person or arouse or to gratify the sexual desire of any person?

3c: Which one?

4a: Did anyone or anything force or coerce you to commit this sexual conduct?

4b: Could you have avoided touching VICTIM if you had wanted to?

4c: Do you believe you have any legal justification or excuse for committing this conduct?
