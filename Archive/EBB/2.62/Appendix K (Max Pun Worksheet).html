APPENDIX K: MAXIMUM PUNISHMENT AND PARAMETER GUILTY PLEA WORKSHEET

NOTE: As part of determining whether the accused’s guilty plea is knowing and voluntary, the judge must ensure the accused understands the maximum punishment under the law pursuant to the accused’s guilty plea, without regard to the terms of any plea agreement. If parameters apply, the judge may also want to ensure the accused understands the offense category and confinement range applicable to each offense to which the accused is pleading guilty. To help facilitate these parts of the guilty plea inquiry, the judge may consider requiring the counsel to complete and submit this worksheet prior to trial. After ensuring its accuracy, the judge should direct that the worksheet be marked as an appellate exhibit and refer to that exhibit when conducting those parts of the guilty plea inquiry.
