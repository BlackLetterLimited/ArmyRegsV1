Article 104 – PUBLIC RECORDS OFFENSES

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you [altered, concealed, removed, mutilated, obliterated, or destroyed] [took with the intent to alter, conceal, remove, mutilate, obliterate, or destroy] a public record, namely: (state the record alleged); and

(2) That the (altering, concealing, removing, mutilating, obliterating or destroying) (__________) (taking) was willful and unlawful.

Definitions:

“Willfully” means intentionally or on purpose.

“Public records” include records, reports, statements, or data compilations in any form, to include paper, microfiche, or computerized, of public offices or agencies, setting forth the activities of the office or agency, or matters observed pursuant to duty imposed by law.

“Public records” includes classified matters.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, did you alter/conceal/remove/mutilate/obliterate/destroy OR take with the intent to alter/conceal/remove/mutilate/obliterate/destroy/steal a public record, namely: (state the record alleged)?

1b: Was this a “public record?”

1c: Where did you get it, or how did you get access to it?

1d: How did you know it was a public record?

1e: What did you do with the record?

1f: Why did you do it?

2a: Was your conduct was willful and unlawful?

2b: Did you do this intentionally to the record?

2c: Did you understand at the time you were doing it that your conduct was unlawful?

2d: Why do you believe your conduct was unlawful?

2e: Did anyone force or coerce you into this conduct?

2f: Could you have avoided this conduct if you had wanted to?

2g: Was it a freely made decision on your part to engage in this conduct?

2h: Do you believe you had any legal justification or authorization?
