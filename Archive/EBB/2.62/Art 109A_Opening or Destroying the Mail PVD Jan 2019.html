Article 109A – OPENING, SECRETING, OR DESTROYING THE MAIL

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you opened, secreted, or destroyed certain mail matter, to wit: (letters) (postal cards) (packages) (_________), addressed to (state the name of the addressee);

(2) That the opening, secreting, or destroying was wrongful; and

(3) That the mail matter was opened, secreted, or destroyed by you before it was delivered to or received by the addressee.

“Wrongful” means without legal justification or excuse.

“Mail matter” means any matter deposited in a postal system of any government or any authorized depository thereof or in official mail channels of the United States or any agency thereof including the armed forces. The value of mail matter is not an element of the offense.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a. On DATE, did you (open/secret/destroy) certain mail?

1b. What did the mail matter contain?

1c. Who was the mail addressed to?

1d. Where did these acts occur?

2a. Was this mail yours?

2b. Was any of it addressed to you?

2c. Did any of the individuals to which the mail was addressed give you authorization to (open)(secret)(destroy) their mail?

2d. Did anyone force or coerce you into doing this?

2e. Could you have avoided doing it if you had wanted to?

2f. Do you feel you have any justification or authorization for doing this?

2g. Do you believe your actions were wrongful?

2h. Why?

3a. Was this mail matter (opened) (secreted) (destroyed) by you before it was delivered to or received by PERSONS?

3b. Describe how you (opened) (secreted) (destroyed) the mail matter.

3c. Was all of the mail sealed and intact when you first came into possession of it?
