Article 120 – ABUSIVE SEXUAL CONTACT WHEN ASLEEP, UNCONSCIOUS, OR OTHERWISE UNAWARE

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused [committed sexual contact upon] [caused ________ to commit sexual contact upon] (state the name of the alleged victim), by (touching) (causing _________ to touch), the (vulva) (penis) (scrotum) (anus) (groin) (breast) (inner thigh) (buttocks) (________) of _______ directly or through the clothing with (______'s (state body part) ((a(state object));

(2) That the accused did so when (state the name of the alleged victim) was asleep, unconscious, or otherwise unaware that the sexual contact was occurring;

(3) That the accused knew or reasonably should have known that (state the name of the alleged victim) was asleep, unconscious, or otherwise unaware that the sexual act was occurring; (and)

(4) That the accused did so with an intent to abuse, humiliate, or degrade any person, or to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On or about DATE, did you commit a sexual contact upon VICTIM?

1b: What specifically did you do?

1c: Did you touch or cause someone to touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, scrotum, anus, groin, breast, inner thigh, or buttocks?

1d: With what part of the body or with what object did you touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc.?

1e. Did you touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc. directly or through his/her clothing?

2a: Was VICTIM aware that this sexual conduct was occurring?

2b: How do you know VICTM was not aware the sexual conduct was occurring?

2c: Do you believe VICTIM was asleep, unconscious, or otherwise aware?

2d: Which one?

3a: Did you know at the time that you committed the sexual conduct that VICTIM was asleep, unconscious, or otherwise aware?

3b: How was VICTIM acting immediately before the sex conduct occurred?

3c: How was VICTIM acting during the sex conduct?

3d: Did you see VICTIM ingest any alcohol or other intoxicants before the sex conduct? When was that?

3e: Do you believe that a reasonable person would have known that VICTIM was asleep, unconscious, or otherwise unaware when the sexual conduct was occurring?

3f: Why do you believe this?

4a: What was your intent in touching VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc.?

4b: Did you specifically intend to abuse, humiliate, or degrade any person or arouse or to gratify the sexual desire of any person?

4c: Which one?

5a: Did anyone or anything force or coerce you to commit this sexual conduct?

5b: Could you have avoided touching VICTIM if you had wanted to?

5c: Do you believe you have any legal justification or excuse for committing this conduct?
