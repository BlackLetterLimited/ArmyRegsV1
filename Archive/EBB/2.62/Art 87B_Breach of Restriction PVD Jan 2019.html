Article 87B – BREACH OF RESTRICTION

Elements:

(1) That (state the name to the person who ordered restriction) ordered you to be restricted to the limits of (state the limits of the restriction alleged);

(2) That (state the name to the person who ordered restriction) was authorized to order this restriction;

(3) That you knew of the restriction and the limits thereof; and

(4) That (state the time and place alleged), you went beyond the limits of the restriction before being released therefrom by proper authority.

Definitions:

“Restriction” is the moral restraint of a person imposed by an order directing a person to remain within certain specified limits.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a. What was your military unit on DATE?

1b. Who placed you on restriction and why?

1c. What unit were you assigned to when you were placed on restriction?

2a. Did CO have the authority to place you on restriction?

2b. How do you know this?

2c. Do you believe that his/her order placing you on restriction was directly related to the maintenance of good order and discipline at PLACE?

3a. What were the limits of the restriction?

3b. How were you notified that you were placed on restriction?

4a. On (DATE), did you go beyond the limits of your restriction? Why? Where did you go?

4b. Were you aware that you were exceeding the boundaries of your restriction?

4c. Did anyone with proper authority give you permission to exceed these limits?

4d. Had you been released from restriction by proper authority on or before DATE?

4d. Did anyone or anything compel you to do so?

4e. Could you have remained within the limits of your restricted area if you had wanted to?
