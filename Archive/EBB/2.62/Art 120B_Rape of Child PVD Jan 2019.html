Article 120b – RAPE OF A CHILD (for offenses occurring on and after

1 January 2019)

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused committed (a) sexual act(s) upon (state the name of the alleged victim), by (penetrating) (intentionally touching, not through the clothing,) (state name of victim)’s (vulva) (penis) (anus) (mouth) with (the accused’s (penis) (state other body part)) (a (state object)); (and)

NOTE 2: Child under the age of 12 alleged. If it is alleged that the victim was under the age of 12, give the following element:

[(2)] That at the time, (state the name of the alleged victim) had not attained the age of 12 years.

NOTE 3: Child who had attained the age of 12, but had not attained the age of 16 alleged. If it is alleged that the victim had attained the age of 12, but had not attained the age of 16, give the following elements:

[(2)] That the accused did so by

(a) using force against (state the name of the alleged victim or other person against whom force was allegedly used), to wit: (state the force used);

(b) threatening or placing (state the name of the alleged victim) in fear;

(c) rendering (state the name of the alleged victim) unconscious;

(d) administering to (state the name of the alleged victim) a drug, intoxicant, or other similar substance; [and]

[(3)] That at the time, (state the name of the alleged victim) had attained the age of 12 years but had not attained the age of 16 years.

NOTE 4: Penetration by object or body part other than penis or intentionally touching unclothed genitalia. If the penetration occurs by something other than the Accused’s penis or if sexual act consists of intentionally touching the unclothed genitalia, add the following element.

[(3) (4)] That the accused did so with an intent to abuse, humiliate, harass, or degrade any person or to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person.

Definitions:

“Child” means any person who has not attained the age of 16 years.

“Sexual act” has the meaning given in Art. 120, except that the term “sexual act” also includes the intentional touching, not through the clothing, of the genitalia of another person who has not attained the age of 16 years with an intent to abuse, humiliate, harass, degrade, or arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person.

The “vulva” is the external genital organs of the female, including the entrance of the vagina and the labia majora and labia minora. “Labia” is the Latin and medically correct term for “lips.”

“Force” means the use of a weapon; the use of such physical strength or violence as is sufficient to overcome, restrain or injure a child; or inflicting physical harm.

(In the case of a parent-child or similar relationship, the use or abuse of parental or similar authority is sufficient to constitute the use of force.)

(The force which caused the alleged victim to engage in the sexual act need not have been applied by the accused to the alleged victim. It is sufficient if the accused applied such force to any person, which thereby caused the alleged victim to engage in the sexual act.)

“Threatening or placing a child in fear” means a communication or action that is of sufficient consequence to cause the child to fear that non-compliance will result in the child or another person being subjected to the action contemplated by the communication or action.

In proving that a person made a threat, it need not be proven that the person actually intended to carry out the threat or had the ability to carry out the threat.

(The person subject to the action contemplated by the threat need not be the alleged victim. It is sufficient if the accused threatened or placed the alleged victim in fear that any person would subject to the action contemplated by the threat, which thereby caused the alleged victim to engage in the sexual act.)

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

[For all cases:]

1a: Sometime during DATE, did you, in fact engage in a sexual act with VICTIM as I have defined the term for you?

1b: Where did this occur?

1c: Who is VICTIM?

1d: Describe what the sexual act was.

1e: Did you (penetrate)(intentionally touch, not through the clothing) VICTIM?

1f: Where on VICTIM? (vulva)(penis)(anus)(mouth)

1g: What (part of your body)(object) did you use?

[For child under age 12]

2a: Do you know how old VICTIM was at the time this sexual act took place?

2b: How did you know how old VICTIM was at the time?

2c: Do you agree that VICTIM, at the time of these acts, was a child who had not yet reached the age of 12?

[For sexual act by using force]

2a: Did you, in fact, use force to either compel or overcome resistance to the sexual act by VICTIM?

2b: Describe the force you used.

a. If weapon used, describe weapon?

b. Physical force/violence used to overcome, restrain or injure child?

c. Inflicted physical harm to child? How?

d. Abuse of parental or similar authority. What relationship do you have with the child? Do you agree you abused your authority over the child?

2c: Do you agree and admit that it was the use of force that made it possible for you to engage in the sexual act with VICTIM?

3a: Do you know how old VICTIM was at the time this sexual act took place?

3b: How did you know how old VICTIM was at the time?

3c: Do you agree that VICTIM, at the time of these acts, was a child who had reached the age of 12, but had not yet reached the age of 16?

[For sexual act by threat or placing in fear]

2a: Prior to committing this sexual act with/upon VICTIM, did you threaten or do something which placed VICTIM in fear?

2b: Describe what you did/said.

2c: Do you believe that VICTIM feared what you did/said would occur?

2d: Do you believe that you were able to commit this sexual act because of the threat you made to VICTIM?

3a: Do you know how old VICTIM was at the time this sexual act took place?

3b: How did you know how old VICTIM was at the time?

3c: Do you agree that VICTIM, at the time of these acts, was a child who had reached the age of 12, but had not yet reached the age of 16?

[For sexual act by rendering child unconscious]

2a: Was VICTIM unconscious before or during the time when this sexual act took place?

2b: How could you tell that VICTIM was unconscious?

2c: Did you do anything to cause VICTIM to become unconscious?

2d: Describe what you did.

2e: Were you aware that someone else did something or gave VICTIM something to cause him/her to become unconscious?

2f: What did the other person do?

2g: Did VICTIM in fact consent to this act which caused him/her to become unconscious? If so, did VICTIM consent to the sexual act which you performed on VICTIM while he/she was unconscious?

3a: Do you know how old VICTIM was at the time this sexual act took place?

3b: How did you know how old VICTIM was at the time?

3c: Do you agree that VICTIM, at the time of these acts, was a child who had reached the age of 12, but had not yet reached the age of 16?

[For sexual act by administration of drug/intoxicant]

2a: Prior to committing this sexual act on/with VICTIM, did you administer a drug, intoxicant or some other substance to VICTIM?

2b: What substance was it?

2c: How did you know what it was? Were you aware of the effect that SUBSTANCE would have on VICTIM when you administered it?

3a: Do you know how old VICTIM was at the time this sexual act took place?

3b: How did you know how old VICTIM was at the time?

3c: Do you agree that VICTIM, at the time of these acts, was a child who had reached the age of 12, but had not yet reached the age of 16?

[For all cases where penetration was by object or body part other than penis or intentionally touching unclothed genitalia]

3a/4a: What was your intent when you participated in or committed this sexual act?

- to gratify the sexual desires of someone? Who?

- to abuse, humiliate, harass or degrade some person? Who?
