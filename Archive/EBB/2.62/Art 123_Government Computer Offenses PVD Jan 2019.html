Article 123 – OFFENSES CONCERNING GOVERNMENT COMPUTERS

Elements:

DISTRIBUTION OF CLASSIFIED INFORMATION OBTAINED FROM GOVERNMENT COMPUTER

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you knowingly accessed a government computer with an unauthorized purpose;

(2) That you , thereby, obtained classified information, to wit: (here describe the classified information generally, in an unclassified manner);

(3) That you had reason to believe the information could be used to injure the United States or benefit a foreign nation; and

(4) That you intentionally (communicated) (delivered) (transmitted) (caused to be communicated/delivered/transmitted) such information to ___________, a person not entitled to receive it.

ACCESSING GOVERNMENT COMPUTER TO OBTAIN CLASSIFIED INFORMATION

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you intentionally accessed a government computer with an unauthorized purpose; and

(2) That you , thereby, obtained, from any such computer, classified or other protected information, to wit: (here describe the classified or other protected information generally, in an unclassified manner).

CAUSING DAMAGE TO A GOVERNMENT COMPUTER

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you knowingly caused transmission of a program, information, code, or command; and;

(2) That you , as a result, intentionally and without authorization, caused damage to a government computer, to wit: (here describe the alleged damage).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

For DISTRIBUTION OF CLASSIFIED INFORMATION OBTAINED FROM GOVERNMENT COMPUTER ask questions 1-4 below.

1a: On DATE at PLACE did you access a government computer?

1b: Explain what happened.

1c: Why did you access the government computer?

1d: Did you have any permission or authority to access the government computer for that purpose?

1e: At the time you accessed the government computer, did you knowingly do so for an unauthorized purpose?

1f: Why do you believe the purpose for which you accessed the computer was unauthorized?

2a: On DATE at PLACE, did you use the government computer to obtain classified information?

2b: Describe generally, in an unclassified manner, the classified information you obtained.

2c: Do you believe and admit that the classified information you obtained is the same information described in the specification?

3a: When you obtained the classified information, did you have reason to believe the information could be used to injure the United States or benefit a foreign nation?

3b: Why did you believe that?

4a: Did you intentionally (communicate) (deliver) (transmit) (cause to be communicated/delivered/transmitted) such information to another person?

4b: Whom?

4c: Were they entitled to receive it?

4d: Did you know at the time they weren’t entitled to receive it?

4e: How did you know that?

4f: Do you have any legal justification or excuse for this misconduct?

4g: Did anyone authorize you to do so?

4h: Was it a freely made decision on your part to engage in this misconduct?

4i: Did anyone or anything force or coerce you into doing this?

4j: Could you have avoided committing this misconduct if you had wanted to do so?

For ACCESSING GOVERNMENT COMPUTER TO OBTAIN CLASSIFIED INFORMATION as questions 1 and 2 below.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, did you access a government computer?

1b: Did you do so for an unauthorized purpose?

1c: Why did you access the government computer?

1d: Do you believe and admit that was an unauthorized purpose?

1e: Why do you believe that was an unauthorized purpose?

1e: Did you specifically intend to access the government computer for an unauthorized purpose?

2a: As a result of accessing the government computer, did you obtain classified or other protected information?

2b: Describe, in a general, unclassified manner, the classified or other protected information you obtained.

2c: Do you believe and admit that, as a result of your accessing the government computer, you obtained the information described in the specification?

2d: Do you have any legal justification or excuse for this misconduct?

2e: Did anyone authorize you to do so?

2f: Was it a freely made decision on your part to engage in this misconduct?

2g: Did anyone or anything force or coerce you into doing this?

2h: Could you have avoided committing this misconduct if you had wanted to do so?

For CAUSING DAMAGE TO A GOVERNMENT COMPUTER as questions 1 and 2 below.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, did you cause the transmission of a program, information, code, or command?

1b: Describe what you did.

1c: Did you do so knowingly?

2a: Did your actions cause damage to a government computer?

2b: Did you specifically intend to damage a government computer when you did this?

2c: Did you have any authorization to transmit the program, information, code, or command?

2d: Describe the damage you caused?

2e: How do you know you caused the damage?

2f: Do you have any legal justification or excuse for this misconduct?

2g: Did anyone authorize you to do so?

2h: Was it a freely made decision on your part to engage in this misconduct?

2i: Did anyone or anything force or coerce you into doing this?

2j: Could you have avoided committing this misconduct if you had wanted to do so?
