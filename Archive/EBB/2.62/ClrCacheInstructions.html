Clear Browser Cache Instructions

To see the EBB updates, you will likely need to delete your browsing data for the updates to take effect.

Steps in Edge:

  • Click on the three dots (Settings and more) in the top right corner.
  • Select "Settings".
  • Click on "Privacy, search, and services": in the left-hand menu.
  • Under "Delete browsing data," click on "Choose what to clear".
  • Choose the time range: from the dropdown menu (All time).
  • Select the checkboxes: next to the types of data you want to clear (Cookies and other site data, Cached images and files).
  • Click "Clear now".

Steps in Chrome:

  • Click the three dots (More) located in the top right corner of the browser window.
  • Click on "Delete browsing data".
  • You can choose the time range for which you want to delete data (All time).
  • Select the types of data you want to remove (Cookies and other site data, Cached images and files).
  • You can select or deselect any of these options as needed.
  • Click the "Clear now" button.
