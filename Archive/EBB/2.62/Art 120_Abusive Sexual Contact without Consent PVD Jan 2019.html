Article 120 – ABUSIVE SEXUAL CONTACT WITHOUT CONSENT

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused [committed sexual contact upon] [caused ________ to commit sexual contact upon] (state the name of the alleged victim), by (touching) (causing _________ to touch), the (vulva) (penis) (scrotum) (anus) (groin) (breast) (inner thigh) (buttocks) (________) of _______ directly or through the clothing with (______'s (state body part) ((a(state object));

(2) That the accused did so without the consent of (state the name of the alleged victim); (and)

(3) That the accused did so with an intent to abuse, humiliate, or degrade any person, or to arouse or gratify the sexual desire of any person.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On or about DATE, did you commit a sexual contact upon VICTIM?

1b: What specifically did you do?

1c: Did you touch or cause someone to touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, scrotum, anus, groin, breast, inner thigh, or buttocks?

1d: With what part of the body or with what object did you touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc.?

1e. Did you touch VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc. directly or through his/her clothing?

2a: Did VICTM agree to this sexual conduct?

2b: How do you know that VICTIM did not agree?

2c: Did VICTM actually say “no”?

2d: What other signs was VICTIM giving that VICTIM did not consent?

2e: Did you believe at the time that VICTIM was consenting?

2f: Why not?

3a: What was your intent in touching VICTIM’s vulva, penis, etc.?

3b: Did you specifically intend to abuse, humiliate, or degrade any person or arouse or to gratify the sexual desire of any person?

3c: Which one?

4a: Did anyone or anything force or coerce you to commit this sexual conduct?

4b: Could you have avoided touching VICTIM if you had wanted to?

4c: Do you believe you have any legal justification or excuse for committing this conduct?
