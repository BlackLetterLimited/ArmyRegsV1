Article 94 – FAILURE TO PREVENT AND SUPPRESS A MUTINY OR SEDITION

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), an offense of (mutiny) (sedition) was being committed in the presence of ##the accused## by (state the description of those engaged in the mutiny or sedition, as alleged); and

(2) That you failed to do your utmost to prevent and suppress the (mutiny) (sedition) by (state the manner alleged).

Definitions:

The elements of the offense of (mutiny) (sedition) are as follows:

“Utmost” means taking those measures to prevent or suppress a (mutiny) (sedition) which may properly be called for by the circumstances of the situation, keeping in mind your (rank and responsibilities) (employment). (When extreme measures are necessary under the circumstances, the use of a dangerous weapon or the taking of life may be justified, providing excessive force is not used.)

Proof that you actually participated in the (mutiny) (sedition) is not required. However, a factfinder would have to be satisfied by legal and competent evidence beyond a reasonable doubt that (service members) (__________) of (__________) were committing (mutiny) (sedition) in your presence and that you failed, in the manner charged, to do your utmost to prevent and suppress the (mutiny) (sedition).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: At or near (place) on or about (date), was an offense of mutiny/sedition being committed in your presence?

1b: Was it mutiny by creating violence or a disturbance? Or was it mutiny by refusing to follow orders or to perform duty? Or was it sedition?

1c: For mutiny by creating violence or a disturbance:

Did the individual(s) create violence or a disturbance?

How so?

Did they intend to usurp or override military authority when they created this violence or disturbance?

1d: For mutiny by refusing to follow orders or to perform duty:

Did the individuals refuse to obey the orders of _________? Or did they refuse to perform their duty(ies)?

Was _______ a person with the authority to give them orders? How so?

Were they required to follow those orders?

What were those orders (or what was their duty)?

Did they act in concert?

Did they have the intent to usurp or override military authority when they acted?

1e: For sedition:

Did the individuals create a (revolt) (violence) (disturbance) against lawful civil authority?

How so?

Did they act in concert?

Did they act with the intent to cause the (overthrow)(destruction)(overthrow and destruction) of lawful civil authority namely (name of lawful civil authority).

2a: Did you fail to do your utmost to prevent and suppress the (mutiny) (sedition)?

2b: Did anyone or anything prevent you from doing your utmost to prevent and suppress the mutiny or sedition?
