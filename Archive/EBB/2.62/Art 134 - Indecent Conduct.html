ELEMENTS

In specification __ of Charge __, you are charged with the offense of indecent conduct, in violation of Article 134, UCMJ. By pleading guilty to this offense, you are admitting that the following elements are true and accurately describe what you did:

That, between on or about DATE and on or about DATE, at or near LOCATION, you wrongfully engaged in certain conduct, to wit: (State the conduct alleged);

That the conduct was indecent; and

That, under the circumstances, your conduct was (of a nature to bring discredit upon the armed forces) (and) (of a nature to bring discredit upon the armed forces).

Definitions

In considering those elements, you should use and understand the following definitions:

“Indecent” means that form of immorality relating to sexual impurity which is grossly vulgar, obscene, and repugnant to common propriety, and tends to excite sexual desire or deprave morals with respect to sexual relations.

(“Conduct prejudicial to good order and discipline” is conduct which causes a reasonably direct and obvious injury to good order and discipline.)

("Service discrediting conduct" is conduct which tends to harm the reputation of the service or lower it in public esteem.)

Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

Do you have any questions about any of them?

Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

Do you believe and admit that the elements (and definitions taken together) correctly describe what you did?

Do you agree that this is the type of conduct which is service discrediting? Why?

Do you agree that the general public would think less of military members if they heard an Air Force member was committing the types of acts you described? Why?

FINAL QUESTIONS
