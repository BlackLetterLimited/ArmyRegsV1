Article 111 – FLEEING THE SCENE OF AN ACCIDENT-SENIOR PASSENGER

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you were a passenger in a vehicle that was involved in an accident;

(2) That you knew that the vehicle had been in an accident; and

(3) That you were the superior commissioned or noncommissioned officer of the driver or commander of the vehicle and wrongfully ordered, caused, or permitted the driver to leave the scene of the accident without (providing assistance to the victim(s) who had been struck (and injured) by the vehicle) (or) (providing identification).

“Accident” includes those situations where there is damage to property other than the driver’s vehicle or injury to someone other than the driver or a passenger in the accused’s vehicle. It also covers accidents caused by the accused, even if the accused’s vehicle does not contact other people, vehicles, or property.

“Wrongful” means without legal justification or excuse.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: Were you a passenger in a vehicle involved in an accident involving a vehicle?

1b: Where did this accident occur?

1c: When did this accident occur?

1d: Who was involved in this accident?

1e: Who was the driver of the vehicles involved in the accident?

1e: How did this accident occur?

1f: Were there any injuries involved in this accident? Who suffered them? What were they?

1g: Were there any damage involved in this accident? What were they?

2a: Did you know that the vehicle had been in an accident? How?

2b: Did you know that damage(s) or injur(ies) resulted from the accident? How?

3a: Where you the superior commissioned or noncommissioned officer of the driver of the vehicle involved in the accident?

3b: Did the driver remain at the scene of the accident after it occurred? Why not?

3c: Did the driver of the vehicle provide identification? Why not?

3d: Did the driver of the vehicle provide assistance to the victim(s) of the accident? Why not?

3e: Did you order, cause, or permit the driver to leave the scene of the accident without providing (assistance/identification)? Why?

3f: Did you believe you had legal justification or excuse to order, cause or permit the driver to leave the scene of the accident without providing (assistance/identification)?

3g: Could you have refrained from issuing this order, causing the driver to leave or permitting the driver to leave?

3h: Did anyone or anything force you to do this?
