Article 112 – INCAPACITATION FOR DUTY FROM DRUNKENESS OR DRUG USE

Elements:

(1) That you had certain duties to perform, to wit: (state the duties alleged);

(2) That (state the time and place alleged), you was incapacitated for the proper performance of such duties;

(3) That such incapacitation was the result of previous indulgence in intoxicating liquor or any drug; (and)

Definitions:

“Duty” means military duty. Every duty which an officer or enlisted person may legally be required by superior authority to execute is necessarily a military duty.

“Incapacitated” means unfit or unable to properly perform duties as a result of previous alcohol consumption or drug use. Illness resulting from previous indulgence is an example of being “unable” to perform duties.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: What were the duties that you were required to perform?

1b: Who assigned you these duties?

1c: When were you required to perform these duties?

1d: How did you know this?

1e: Did you know of these duties before you became incapacitated?

2a: On or about (date) were you incapacitated as I defined the term for you?

2b: Was this when you were supposed to be performing your duties?

2c: As a result of this incapacitation were you unable to perform your duties?

3a: How did you become incapacitated?

3b: What and how much did you exactly consume?

3c: Was this a freely made decision on your part to consume this (alcohol)(drug)

3d: Could you have avoided consuming this (alcohol)(drug) if you had wanted to?

3e: Do you believe that you have any legal justification or excuse for your actions?
