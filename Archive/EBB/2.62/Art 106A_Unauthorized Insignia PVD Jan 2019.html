Article 106A – WEARING UNAUTHORIZED INSIGNIA, DECORATION, BADGE, RIBBON, DEVICE, OR LAPEL BUTTON

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you wore upon your (uniform) (civilian clothing) (Medal of Honor) (Distinguished Service Cross) (Navy Cross) (Air Force Cross) (Silver Star) (Purple Heart) (a valor device on (name the personal award)) (the insignia of the grade of (a master sergeant of __________) (the Combat Infantryman Badge) (the lapel button representing the Legion of Merit) (__________);

(2) That you were not authorized to wear the (identify the insignia, decoration, or badge alleged); and

(3) That the wearing was wrongful.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE, were you in uniform or civilian clothes?

1b: Were you wearing an item on your uniform/civilian clothes that was unauthorized?

1c: What was it?

1d: How did you wear it?

1e: Why did you wear it?

2a: When you wore the UNAUTHORIZED ITEM, did you believe you were authorized to wear it?

2b: Did you know that you were not authorized to wear it?

2c: Are you aware that the Navy/Marine Corps has uniform regulations?

2d: Have you reviewed these regulations to learn what was authorized to wear on your uniform?

2e: Either through your military training or reviewing the uniform regulations, on DATE, did you understand what you were authorized to wear and what you weren’t?

2f: Did anyone give you any authorization to wear the item?

3a: Do you believe your conduct in wearing the UNAUTHORIZED ITEM was wrongful?

3b: Why?

3c: Did anyone force or coerce you into wearing the item?

3d: Could you have avoided wearing the item on your uniform/civilian clothes if you had wanted to?

3e: Was it a freely made decision on your part to wear the item?
