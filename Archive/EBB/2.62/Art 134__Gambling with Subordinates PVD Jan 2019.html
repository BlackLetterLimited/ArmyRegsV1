Article 134 – GAMBLING WITH SUBORDINATE

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you gambled with (state name and rank or grade of the person alleged);

(2) That you were a noncommissioned or petty officer at the time;

(3) That (state name and rank or grade of the person alleged) was not then a noncommissioned or petty officer and was subordinate to you ;

(4) That you knew that (state name and rank or grade of the person alleged) was not then a noncommissioned or petty officer and was subordinate to (him) (her); and

(5) That, under the circumstances, the conduct of you were (to the prejudice of good order and discipline in the armed forces) (and) (of a nature to bring discredit upon the armed forces).

Definitions:

Conduct prejudicial to good order and discipline is conduct that causes a reasonably direct and obvious injury to good order and discipline.

Service discrediting conduct is conduct that tends to harm the reputation of the service or lower it in public esteem.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: At the time and place alleged, did you gamble with PERSON?

1b: What was PERSON’s rank/rate at that time?

1c: Where did this occur?

1d: How did you come to be gambling?

1e: Was anyone else present?

1f: What kind of gambling was involved?

1g: Was money used?

1h: Did you understand that you were gambling?

2a: Were you a noncommissioned officer or petty officer at the time?

2b: What was your rank/rate?

2c: When had you advanced to that rank?

3a. Was PERSON, at that time, a noncommissioned or petty officer?

3b: Was (s)he subordinate to you?

3c: What was his/her rank on DATE?

3d: Explain how (s)he was subordinate to you?

4a: Did you know that PERSON was not then a noncommissioned or petty officer and was subordinate to you?

4b: How did you know this?

4c: Was this individual in your command?

5a: Under the circumstances, was your conduct prejudicial to good order and discipline in the armed forces or of a nature to bring discredit upon the armed forces?

5b: In what way?

5c: Did anyone force or coerce you into gambling with PERSON?

5d: Was it a freely made decision on your part?

5e: Could you have avoided gambling with a subordinate if you had wanted?

5f: Did you understand your conduct was wrongful?

5g: Do you believe you had any legal authorization or justification for your conduct?
