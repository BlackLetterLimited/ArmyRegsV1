Article 104B – EFFECTING UNLAWFUL ENLISTMENT, APPOINTMENT OR SEPARATION

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you effected the (enlistment) (appointment) (separation) of (state the name of the person allegedly unlawfully enlisted, appointed, or separated) (in) (from) the United States (Navy)(Marine Corps) (__________);

(2) That (state the name of the person allegedly unlawfully enlisted, appointed, or separated) was ineligible for this (enlistment) (appointment) (separation) because it was prohibited by (law) (regulation) (order), as described in the specification; and

(3) That you knew of the ineligibility at the time (he) (she) caused or brought about the (enlistment) (appointment) (separation).

Definitions:

“Enlistment” means a voluntary entry or enrollment for a specific term of service in one of the Armed Forces by any person except a commissioned or warrant officer.

“Appointment” means any method by which a commissioned or warrant officer enters into the service of an Armed Force.

“Separation” means any method by which a member of an Armed Force is released from the service.

“Release from the service” includes any severance or disconnection from an active or inactive duty status.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE did you work at COMMAND?

1b: On DATE at PLACE did you effect the enlistment/appointment/separation of PERSON?

1c: Did PERSON come to see you about joining or separating from the SERVICE?

1d: Was it your job to screen individuals for enlistment/appointment/separation?

1e: Did your job require you to fill out paperwork and/or make recommendations for enlistment/appointment/separation?

1f: On DATE in PLACE, did you effect the (enlistment) (appointment) (separation) of PERSON?

2a: Was PERSON ineligible for enlistment/appointment/separation because it was prohibited by law, regulation, or order?

2b: What law, regulation, or order?

3a: Did you know of this law, regulation, or order on DATE?

3b: Was it part of your job to know of laws, regulations, or orders pertaining to enlistment/separation/appointment of service members?

3c: On DATE did you know PERSON was ineligible for enlistment/appointment/separation?

3d: How did you know?

3e: Could you have avoided effecting this enlistment/appointment/separation if you had wanted to?

3f: Did anyone force or coerce you into effecting this enlistment/appointment/separation?

3g: Was it a freely made decision on your part to do this?
