3-20-1. RELEASING PRISONER WITHOUT AUTHORITY (ARTICLE 96)

a. MAXIMUM PUNISHMENT: DD, TF, 2 years, E-1.

b. SAMPLE SPECIFICATION:

In that __________ (personal jurisdiction data) did, (at/on board--location), on or about __________, without proper authority release __________, a prisoner committed to his/her charge.

c. ELEMENTS:


(1) That (state the name of the prisoner alleged to have been released) was a prisoner committed to the charge of the accused; and

(2) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused released the prisoner without proper authority.

d. DEFINITIONS AND OTHER INSTRUCTIONS:


“Prisoner” refers to a person who is physically restrained because of confinement or custody. “Release” refers to an unauthorized removal of restraint by the custodian, rather than by the prisoner, under circumstances which demonstrate to the prisoner that (he) (she) is no longer in legal (confinement) (custody).
