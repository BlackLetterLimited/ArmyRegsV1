Article 86 – ABANDONING WATCH

Elements:

(1) That the accused was a member of the (guard) (watch) (duty section) at (state the time and place alleged);

(2) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused went from or remained absent from (his) (her) (guard) (watch) (duty section);

(3) That this absence was without proper authority; and

(4) That the accused intended to abandon (his) (her) (guard) (watch) (duty section).

Definitions:

“Intended to abandon” means that you, at the time the absence began or at some time during the absence, must have intended to completely separate yourself from all further responsibility for your particular duty as a member of the (guard) (watch) (duty section).

“Duty section” describes a group of personnel who have been designated to remain within the limits of a military (vessel) (command) during those times, such as liberty hours, when personnel strength is below normal, in order to accomplish the mission and ensure the safety of the (vessel) (command).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a. Were you a member of a guard, watch, or duty section on DATE?

1b. Explain.

1c. Did you have a duty to be with (guard) (watch) (duty section) on that date? How did you know?

2a. Were you present at your (guard)(watch)(duty section)on DATE? Where were you?

2b. How did your absence occur?

2c. Why weren't you present?

2d. Was it a freely made decision on your part to absent yourself?

2e. Could you have been present if you had wanted to?

2f. Did you realize at the time that you were abandoning your (guard)(watch)(duty section)?

3a. Did you have permission from anyone in military authority to be absent from your (guard)(watch)(duty section)?

3b. Did you think you had permission from anyone to be absent?

4a. Did you intend to abandon your (guard) (watch) (duty section)?

4b. Why did you abandon it?

3c. Did you have any physical or mental infirmity that prevented you from going to/staying at your (guard) (watch)(duty section)?
