Article 124A – BRIBERY-PROMISING, OFFERING, OR GIVING

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you wrongfully and unlawfully (promised) (offered) (gave) (the sum of __________ dollars) (__________ of a value of about __________ dollars) to (state the name of the person alleged);

(2) That, at that time, (state the name of the person alleged) (occupied an official position) (had official duties), namely, (state the official position or official duties as alleged);

(3) That this (sum) (__________) was (promised) (offered) (given) with the intent to influence the (decision) (action) of (state the name of the person alleged) with respect to (state the matter alleged; and

(4) That (state the matter alleged) was an official matter in which the United States was interested.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE did you engage in the offense of bribery?

1b: Did you offer, promise, or give money or something of value?

1c: What did you offer, promise, or give?

1d: What was its value?

1e: Who was the person to whom you offered, promised, or gave money or something of value?

1f: What was your relationship to that other person?

1g: Do you agree that your conduct in offering, promising, or giving money or something of value to PERSON was unlawful and wrongful?

2a: What was/were PERSON’S title/official position/duties at that time?

2b: Did you know that at the time?

2c: How did you know that?

2d: Where/how did this talk/meeting take place?

2e: What transpired?

3a: Why did you offer, promise, or give the money or article of value to PERSON?

3b: When you offered, promised, or gave the money or item of value to PERSON, did you specifically intend to influence PERSON in regard to ALLEGED MATTER?

3c: Explain why you believe that.

4a: Do you believe and admit that ALLEGED MATTER was an official matter in which the United States was and is interested?

4b: Why do you believe that?

4c: What, if any, authorization or legal justification did you have for this conduct?

4d: Do you believe and admit that your conduct was wrongful?

4e: Did anyone or anything give you permission or authority to do this?

4f: Did anyone or anything force you to do this?

4g: Could you have avoided doing this if you had wanted to?
