Article 112A – DRUGS-WRONGFUL DISTRIBUTION

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), the accused distributed __________ (grams) (ounces) (pounds) (__________), more or less of (__________) (a Schedule ___ controlled substance);

(2) That the accused actually knew (he) (she) distributed the substance;

(3) That the accused actually knew that the substance (he) (she) distributed was (__________) (or of a contraband nature); (and)

(4) That the distribution by the accused was wrongful; [and]

NOTE 1: Aggravating circumstance alleged. If one of the aggravating factors in Article 112a is pled, the military judge must also instruct on that aggravating factor as an element:

[(5)] That at the time the accused distributed the substance as alleged, (it was a time of war) (the accused was (on duty as a sentinel or lookout) (on board a vessel or aircraft used by or under the control of the armed forces) (in or at a missile launch facility used by the armed forces or under the control of the armed forces) (receiving special pay under 37 U.S. Code section 310)).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: (On)(Between) DATE ALLEGED, did you distribute DRUG?

[Divers occasions] How often did you distribute it during this time period?

1b. Where were you when you distributed this substance?

1c. How much did you distribute? How do you know it was this amount?

1d. Do you agree that DRUG is a schedule (I) (II) (III) (IV) (V) controlled Substance under the Comprehensive Drug Abuse Prevention and Control Act of 1970 (21 U.S.C. sects. 801-971)?

1e. Have you discussed this act with your defense counsel?

1f. After that discussion, are you satisfied that DRUG appears in schedule (I) (II) (III) (IV) (V) of that act?

2a. Did you realize at the time that you distributed this substance?

2b. “Distribute” means to deliver something to the possession of another. How did you deliver DRUG to the possession of another?

2c. Who did you distribute DRUG to?

2d. Did you deliver it to this person in exchange for money or other promises?

3a. At the time did you realize that the substance you distributed was DRUG?

3b. How did you know it was DRUG?

3c. What did it look like?

3d. How did you obtain this substance?

3e. What, if anything, did the person who gave it to you, say about this substance?

3f. Was a portion of the substance ever seized by authorities? If so, was it ever tested?

3g. Have you and your counsel seen the lab results concerning the test?

3h. Are you satisfied that based on those lab results that the substance was DRUG?

4a. Did you have any authority to distribute DRUG?

4b. Did anyone force you to distribute it?

4c. Could you have avoided distributing it if you wanted to?

4d. Do you believe and admit that your distribution of it was wrongful, that is, without any legal justification or excuse?
