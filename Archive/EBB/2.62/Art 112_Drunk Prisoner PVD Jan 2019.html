Article 112 – DRUNK PRISONER

Elements:

(1) That you were a prisoner; and

(2) That (state the time and place alleged), and while in such status, (he/she) was found drunk.

A “prisoner” is a person who is in confinement or custody imposed pursuant to lawful apprehension, pre-trial restraint, or pre-trial confinement, or by the sentence of a court-martial, who has not been set free by a person with authority to release the prisoner.

“Drunk” means the state of intoxication by alcohol that is sufficient to impair the rational and full exercise of mental or physical faculties or the state of meeting or exceeding a blood alcohol content limit with respect to alcohol concentration in a person's blood of 0.08 grams of alcohol per 100 milliliters of blood and with respect to alcohol concentration in a person's breath of 0.08 grams of alcohol per 210 liters of breath, as shown by chemical analysis.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On _____ were you in confinement or custody?

1b: How did your confinement/custody come about?

1c: Had you been set free by a person with the authority to release you?

1d: At that time, did you believe that you remained in confinement or custody?

2a: On ____ were you intoxicated?

2b: Was your intoxication a result of alcohol use? How much alcohol did you drink?

2c: Was there a breathalyzer conducted (or other blood alcohol test administered)? Do you know the results of that test?

2d: At the time, was your state of intoxication sufficient to impair the rational and full exercise of your mental or physical faculties?

2e: In what way/manner was your impairment shown?

2e: Do you believe that you were drunk at the time? Why?

2f: How did your intoxication become known?

2g: Do believe that you had any legal justification or excuse to be drunk on duty?

2h: Did anyone or anything force you to become drunk?

2i: Do you believe that you could have refrained from being intoxicated/drunk while on duty?
