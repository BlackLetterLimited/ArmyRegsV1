Article 132 – DISCOURAGING A REPORT

Elements:

(1) That (state the time and place alleged), you wrongfully (took or threatened to take adverse personnel action against ________ to wit: ____________) (withheld or threatened to withhold a favorable personnel action with respect to _________ to wit: _____________); and

(2) That, at the time of the action, the accused intended to discourage ______________ from (reporting a criminal offense) (making a protected communication).

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE at PLACE did you take or threaten adverse action against VICTIM, or did you withhold or threaten to withhold favorable action?

1b: Who was VICTIM?

1c: At the time, what was your relationship with VICTIM?

1d: Describe what you did to take or threaten unfavorable action or to withhold or threaten to withhold favorable action.

1e: Do you believe and admit that your conduct was wrongful?

1f: Did you have any legal justification or excuse for your conduct?

1g: Could you have avoided threatening, taking, or withholding the action if you had wanted to?

1h: Was the decision to retaliate in this manner freely made on your part?

2a: Why did you take or threaten to take or withhold or threaten to withhold action as you did?

2b: Did you specifically intend to discourage VICTIM from reporting a criminal offense or making a protected communication?

2c: At the time were did you believe VICTIM was planning to or might make a protected communication or report a criminal offense?

2d: Describe the circumstances that led to you discouraging VICTIM from making a protected communication or reporting a criminal offense.
