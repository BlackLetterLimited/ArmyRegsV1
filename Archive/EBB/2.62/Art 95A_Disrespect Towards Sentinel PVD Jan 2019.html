Article 95A – DISRESPECT TOWARDS SENTINEL OR LOOKOUT

Elements:

(1) That (state name of the sentinel or lookout alleged) was a (sentinel) (lookout) (state the time and place alleged);

(2) That you knew that (state name of the sentinel or lookout alleged) was a (sentinel) (lookout);

(3) That (state the time and place alleged), you [used disrespectful language, to wit: (state the disrespectful language alleged)] [behaved in a disrespectful manner, to wit: (state the disrespectful behavior alleged)]

(4) That the (use of such language) (behavior) by you were wrongful;

(5) That the (language) (behavior) was directed toward and within the sight or hearing of (state name of the sentinel or lookout alleged); and

(6) That (state name of the sentinel or lookout alleged) was at the time in the execution of his/her duties as a (sentinel) (lookout).

Definitions:

A “sentinel” or a “lookout” is a person whose duties include the requirement to maintain constant alertness, be vigilant, and remain awake, in order to observe for the possible approach of the enemy, or to guard persons, property, or a place and to sound the alert, if necessary.

A (sentinel) (lookout) is in the execution of his/her duties when doing any act or service required or authorized to be done by him/her by (statute) (regulation) (the order of a superior) (or) (by custom of the service).

“Disrespectful” means behavior or language which detracts from the respect due to the authority of a (sentinel) (lookout).

"Wrongful" means without any legal justification or excuse.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: Was (name of service member) a sentinel or lookout on (date) at (place)?

2a: Did you know there were a (sentinel) (lookout)?

2b: How did you know?

3a: Did you use disrespectful language or behave in a disrespectful manner?

3b: Which one?

3c: What did you say? Or what did you do?

3d: How was that disrespectful?

4a: Was your (language) (behavior) wrongful?

4b: Did anyone force or coerce you to use this language or behave in this manner?

4c: Could you have avoided (using this language) (behaving in this manner) if you had wanted to?

5: Was your (language) (behavior) directed toward or in the sight of (state the name of the lookout or sentinel)?

6: Was (name of lookout or sentinel) in the execution of his or her duties at the time?
