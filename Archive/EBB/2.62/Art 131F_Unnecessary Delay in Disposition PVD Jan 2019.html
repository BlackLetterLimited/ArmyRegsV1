Article 131F – UNNECESSARY DELAY IN DISPOSING OF CASE

Elements:

(1) That you were charged with the duty of (state the duty alleged) in connection with the disposition of the case of (state the name of the person alleged), a person accused under the Uniform Code of Military Justice;

(2) That you knew that (he) (she) was charged with this duty;

(3) That (state the time and place alleged), delay occurred in the disposition of the case;

(4) That you were responsible for the delay; and

(5) That, under the circumstances, the delay was unnecessary.

Definitions:

A person is responsible for delay in the disposition of a case only when that person’s duties require action with respect to the disposition of that case.

Inquiry:

0a: Are you currently on active duty in the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps?

0b: On [DATE OF EARLIEST OFFENSE] were you a member of the U.S. Navy/U.S. Marine Corps on active duty?

0c: Have you been discharged or released from active duty since that date?

0d: In the specification is your name, rank, rate, unit, and branch of service correctly stated and spelled?

0e: Do you understand the elements and definitions as I have read them to you?

0f: Do you have any questions about any of them?

0g: Do you understand that your plea of guilty admits that these elements accurately describe what you did?

0h: Do you believe and admit that the elements and definitions taken together correctly describe what you did?

0i: Tell me in your own words why you believe you are guilty of this offense.

1a: On DATE, at PLACE, did you have certain duties with respect to the disposition of a case involving PERSON?

1b: Describe what those duties were?

1c: Who charged you with this duty in relation to the case against PERSON?

1d: Did that person permit or allow you to delay your duties in this case?

2a: Did you know you had these particular duties?

2b: How did you learn of your duties in relation to this case?

2c: Were you told or advised that there were specific time frames in which you were to conduct your duties?

2d: Describe what your duties were and the time in which you were to have completed them.

3a: Did delay occur in this case?

3b: Describe the delay that occurred in the disposition of the case?

4a: Do you believe that you were responsible for the delay that occurred in the disposition of this case? Why?

4b: What, if anything did you do to cause/permit/fail to prevent the delay?

4c: Could you have avoided causing/permitting/failing to prevent the delay to occur in this case if you had wanted to?

5a: Do you believe the delay that occurred in this case was unnecessary?

5b: Why?

5c: Do you believe your conduct was wrongful?

5d: Did anyone force or coerce you into this conduct?

5e: Do you believe you had any legal justification or excuse for permitting/causing/failing to prevent the delay in the case of PERSON?

5f: Could you have prevented this delay if you had wanted to?
