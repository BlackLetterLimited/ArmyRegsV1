. U.S. and foreign unit awards a. Where worn. See DA Pam 670–1. b. Fourrageres and lanyards. Permanent and temporary fourrageres and lanyards may be worn on the service/dress uniform per the provisions of AR 600–8–22. Only one fourragere, lanyard, aiguillette, or cord is authorized for wear on each shoulder. c. Foreign unit awards. If a foreign unit award is worn, personnel must wear at least one other U.S. decoration, service medal, or unit award. Foreign unit awards are worn after U.S. unit awards, by date of receipt.
