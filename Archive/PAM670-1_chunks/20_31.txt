. Umbrella a. Type. The umbrella is an optional purchase item. b. Description. The umbrella is black, plain with no logos or designs, and of a commercial design. c. How worn. Soldiers may carry and use an umbrella only during inclement weather when wearing the service uniforms (Class A and Class B), dress variations, and mess uniforms. Umbrellas are not authorized in formations or when wearing field or utility uniforms. Commanders may further restrict Soldiers’ use of umbrellas, as appropriate.
