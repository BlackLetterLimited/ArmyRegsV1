Monitor the Heraldic Quality Control Program in accordance with AR 672–8, to ensure heraldic items are manufactured according to Government specifications or purchase descriptions. b. Provide manufacturers with Government-loaned tools and specifications for heraldic items. AR 670–1 • 26 January 2021 2 c. Authorize the manufacture of heraldic items and issue certificates of authority to manufacture items in accordance with the provisions of AR 672–8. d. Approve designs for distinctive unit insignia (DUI), regimental distinctive insignia (RDI), shoulder sleeve insignia (SSI), flags, and badges, as authorized by this regulation. e.
