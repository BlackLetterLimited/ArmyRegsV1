. Authorization for wear The ASU–M is authorized for year-round wear by pregnant Soldiers as a dress uniform, when prescribed for wear by CTA 50–900, AR 700–84, and the commander. The ASU–M is a supplemental issue item for enlisted Soldiers; it is a personally procured item for officers. See DA Pam 670–1 for uniforms currently classified as ASU–M, and for authorized variations of the uniform.
