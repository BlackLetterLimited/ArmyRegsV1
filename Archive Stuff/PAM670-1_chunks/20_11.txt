. Cufflinks and studs a. Type. Cufflinks and studs are optional purchase items. b. Description. Male Soldiers may wear the cufflinks and studs with the following uniforms: (1) Blue and white evening mess uniform. Personnel may wear plain white studs or cufflinks, such as white motherof-pearl, with or without rims of platinum or white gold. (2) Blue and white mess uniforms. Personnel may wear gold or gold-colored metal studs or cufflinks with a round, plain face. The cufflinks may be post or link type, 1/2 to 3/4 inch in diameter. Studs may be 1/4 to 3/8 inch in diameter. (3) Army service uniform dress variation. If worn, the cufflinks will be plain gold or gold-colored metal as described in paragraph 20–11b(2).
