. General guidelines a. The AGSU–M is intended for wear during most duty or nonfield occasions and for travel and off-duty purposes (see app D for possession date). While both the Class A and B AGSU–Ms are authorized for year-round wear, the appropriate uniform is worn based on weather conditions, duties, and the formality of the occasion. When a specific service uniform is not prescribed for formations or other occasions when uniformity in appearance is not required, Soldiers may wear the Class A AGSU–M or any of the variations of the Class B AGSU–M with the accessories authorized for these uniforms.
